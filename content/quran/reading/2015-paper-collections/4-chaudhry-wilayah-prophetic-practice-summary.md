# Ayesha S. Chaudhry: Producing Gender-Egalitarian Islamic Law — A Case Study of Guardianship (*Wilayah*) in Prophetic Practice

## 基本信息

- **作者**: Ayesha S. Chaudhry
- **出处**: *Men in Charge? Rethinking Authority in Muslim Legal Tradition*, ed. Ziba Mir-Hosseini et al., London: Oneworld, 2015
- **页码范围**: 153–180
- **关键词**: 圣行（Sunnah）、圣训（hadith）、婚姻监护权（wilayah）、同意（consent）、伊斯兰女性主义法学方法论

---

## 一、核心论点

Chaudhry 主张：穆斯林女性主义学者不能忽视先知实践（Sunnah），因为**任何忽略圣行的性别平等伊斯兰愿景在理论和实践上都将无效**。她通过对婚姻监护权（wilayah）相关圣训的案例研究，展示如何从圣训文本中挖掘反父权制的规范性内容，为性别平等的伊斯兰法律改革提供宗教权威基础。

> "Any gender-egalitarian vision of Islam will be ineffective both theoretically and practically if it ignores prophetic practice." (p. 158)

> "Since prophetic practice is considered a foundational source of Islamic law, any serious and comprehensive feminist engagement with the Islamic tradition must address this source." (p. 158)

---

## 二、方法论框架

### 1. 为何必须面对圣行？

Chaudhry 指出穆斯林女性主义者迄今几乎**完全集中于古兰经文本**，而系统地回避了圣训文献。她分析了三个原因：

**(a) 古兰经的权威地位**：古兰经被视为真主的言辞，具有普遍适用性，将性别平等愿景锚定于此最为有力。

> "The Qur'an is considered to be the literal word of God by mainstream Muslims today. Since it is considered to be universally applicable, it makes sense to root a gender-egalitarian vision of Islam in the Qur'anic text." (p. 154)

**(b) 圣训文献的复杂性**：与古兰经不同，圣训数量庞大、作者众多，且每条圣训都包含传述链（isnad）和正文（matn），其真实性始终是争议焦点。

> "Whereas the Qur'an is only one volume and can be physically held in one's hand, prophetic reports figure in the hundreds of thousands, span many volumes and have multiple authors." (p. 155)

**(c) 先知的历史处境**：先知穆罕默德无法像古兰经那样被从其父权制语境中"解放"出来——他的言行始终与具体历史事件直接关联。

> "Prophet Muhammad cannot be liberated from his context; his words and actions are always directly connected to historical events that sometimes reflect and sometimes subvert his context." (p. 156)

### 2. 面对圣行的两种路径

Chaudhry 排除了将先知描绘为"女性主义者"的时代错置做法，转而主张：

> "The other option is to accept Prophet Muhammad in the patriarchal context in which he lived, all the while looking for indications of his resisting patriarchy in even the smallest ways possible." (p. 158)

她将这些"小型抵抗行为"解读为先知与神圣启示互动的**反父权制影响**：

> "It is precisely in these moments that the gender-egalitarian interpretations of the Qur'an by Muslim feminists are most fully supported, because these moments can be read as the counter-patriarchal influence of Prophet Muhammad's interaction with the divine in prophetic practice." (p. 159)

### 3. 选择性引用的合法性——教法学传统的先例

Chaudhry 为"有目的地选择性阅读圣训"进行辩护，指出**前殖民时期的经注学家和法学家本身就是选择性引用圣训**来支持特定立场的：

> "When Qur'an commentators and jurists cited prophetic reports to support their particular interpretations of the Qur'anic text, they often drew only on the texts of prophetic reports, regardless of whether the chain of transmission was weak or strong." (p. 159)

> "The chain of transmission of a prophetic report usually came into play only when a scholar wanted to debunk another scholar's position or interpretation." (p. 159)

**具体例证——古兰经 4:34 的解经史**：在丈夫管教妻子的问题上，经注学家和法学家有选择地引用强调丈夫权利的圣训，而**无人引用**阿伊莎关于先知从未打过任何人的报告，也无人引用先知诅咒反复打妻子之人的圣训：

> "No scholar cited the prophetic report in which Prophet Muhammad's youngest wife 'Aisha reported that Prophet Muhammad never hit anyone – not a child, woman, or slave – in his life, except when fighting in war; or the report in which Prophet Muhammad cursed a man who repeatedly hit his wife." (p. 160)

因此：

> "The malleability of prophetic practice is not new, nor is the methodology of selective reading; it is the gender-egalitarian principles guiding the reading, as well as the self-conscious and forthright nature of this reading, that is modern and creative." (p. 160)

### 4. 借鉴的学术先驱

| 学者 | 方法 |
|---|---|
| **Kecia Ali (2004)** | 将先知言行分为"典范性的"（exemplary）与"例外性的"（exceptional），使信众可以在先知实践符合当代伦理时效仿、不符合时搁置而非否定 (p. 161) |
| **Lynda Clarke (2003)** | 质问"为何自由派实际上放弃了圣训，将阵地拱手让给对手" (p. 161) |
| **Sa'diyya Shaikh (2004)** | 以主体性自觉的方式解读布哈里圣训集"知识之书"中的圣训 (p. 161) |

## 三、案例研究一：同意问题（The Problem of Consent）

Chaudhry 分析了三条关于婚姻监护权与女性同意的圣训（均出自 Abu Dawud, *Kitab al-Sunan*, 1998, vol. 3）：

### 圣训一：关于孤女的征询

基本版本：

> "Ask permission [*tusta'maru*] from the orphan girl [*yatima*] with regard to herself. If she is silent [*sakatat*] then this is her permission/consent [*fa-huwa idhnuha*], but if she refuses [*abat*] [her guardian] has no licence/authority [*jawaz*] against her." (Abu Dawud, vol. 3, p. 25; 引于 p. 163)

**变体一**：阿伊莎将"孤女"（yatima）替换为"处女"（bikr），向先知说"处女感到害羞/难为情（*tastahyi*）不好意思开口"，先知回答"她的沉默就是她的同意（*suktuha iqraruha*）"。(p. 163)

**变体二**：先知说女性的"沉默"（sakatat）和"哭泣"（bakat）都是她接受的表示。(p. 164)

### 圣训二：关于已婚妇女（thayyib）与处女

> "The widow [*al-ayyim*] has a greater right over herself than her guardian [*waliyyuha*], and the virgin [*bikr*] should be asked permission about herself, and her silence is her permission [*wa-idhnuha samtuha*]." (Abu Dawud, vol. 3, p. 26; 引于 p. 164)

**教法学推导**：已婚/离异/丧偶女性对自己拥有比其监护人更大的权利，不需要监护人即可缔结婚约；处女则需通过监护人嫁出，但必须征求其意见。

### 圣训三：汉莎·宾特·希达姆的案例（行为叙事）

> "The Ansari woman, Khansa bt. Khidham, reported that her father married her when she was a divorcee/widow [*thayyib*] and she disliked this, so she went to the Messenger of God and mentioned this to him, so he nullified [*radda*] her marriage." (Abu Dawud, vol. 3, p. 27; 引于 p. 164)

**类似案例**：一名处女也因父亲未经许可将其嫁出，向先知投诉后被解除婚约。(p. 164–165)

### 法学分析：挑战与机遇

**父权制预设**：三条圣训的基本性别假设都是父权制的——男性担任监护人，女性按经济/社会独立程度和性经验来分类。(p. 165)

**反父权制突破**：

1. **限制监护人权力**：圣训要求征询孤女/处女意见，不得违背其意愿嫁她。
   > "The reports limit the guardian's authority and assert the agency of women." (p. 165)

2. **"沉默即同意"原则的内在张力**：Chaudhry 指出沉默可能源于害羞、义务感或压力，将沉默甚至哭泣视为同意会助长强迫婚姻。(p. 165)

3. **汉莎圣训的深远法律后果**——使监护权变得近乎无关紧要：
   > "If a woman can claim at any point, during or after her marriage, that she was married against her will, then she has grounds for nullifying the marriage." (p. 167)

   这极大地拓展了女性获得离婚的途径：
   > "This greatly increases a woman's access to divorce, because instead of proving that she deserves divorce for complex reasons she can simply assert that she was forced into an unwanted marriage." (p. 167)

4. **同意之不可证明性反而有利于女性**：既然无法证明沉默确实等于同意，女性可以事后主张自己的沉默并非同意、其同意受到胁迫、或监护人根本未征询她的意见。(p. 167)

5. **女性言辞的法律效力贯穿婚姻始终**：
   > "The legal weight of a woman's word over the life of the marriage, even after the marriage has been successfully contracted, lends prophetic authority to developing legal reforms that increase women's agency in both marriage and divorce." (p. 167–168)

**局限**：这些圣训未能解决未成年人（孤儿和/或处女）婚姻问题，但为**禁止强迫婚姻**和**增加女性在婚姻与离婚中的能动性**提供了法律改革基础。(p. 168)

## 四、案例研究二：孤儿与多妻制（Orphans and Polygamy）

Chaudhry 分析了一条记录阿伊莎与其侄儿乌尔瓦·本·祖拜尔对话的圣训（出自 *Sahih Bukhari*, 1979, vol. 4, p. 20），阿伊莎就古兰经 4:3 和 4:127 两节经文提供经注。

### 圣训内容

乌尔瓦问阿伊莎关于古兰经 4:3（"如果你们担心不能公正对待孤女，那就娶你们喜欢的女性……"）的含义。

阿伊莎的解释：

> "This is about the orphan girl who lives in her guardian's home. He is attracted by her beauty [*jamaliha*] and wealth [*maliha*] and wants to marry her with less than appropriate [dower (*mahr*)] for a woman of her status. [Such men] are prohibited from marrying [such women] unless they can be equitable [*yuqsitu*] to [the women] in providing the total dower [*sadaq*]." (Sahih Bukhari, vol. 4, p. 20; 引于 p. 168–169)

阿伊莎随后引用古兰经 4:127 作为进一步解释：

> "Just as [guardians] would leave the [orphan girl] when they are unattracted to her, they should not marry her when they are attracted to her, unless they will be equitable/just with her by completing her dower and giving her her rights." (Sahih Bukhari, vol. 4, p. 20; 引于 p. 169)

### 阿伊莎的经注方法论意义

阿伊莎采用的是**以经解经**（*tafsir al-Qur'an bi-l-Qur'an*）方法——用古兰经 4:127 来解释 4:3。

> "In explaining specific Qur'anic verses through the use of other Qur'anic verses (*tafsir al-Qur'an bi-l-Qur'an*), modern Muslim feminists emulate the methodology of 'Aisha." (p. 170)

**关键发现**：不同的互文连接导致完全不同的法律结论：

| 连接方式 | 经文焦点 | 法律含义 |
|---|---|---|
| 4:3 ↔ 4:127（阿伊莎方法） | 公正对待孤女 | 限制监护人权力、保障孤女聘礼权利 |
| 4:3 ↔ 4:129（当代女性主义方法） | 多妻制 | 因永远无法公正对待多妻而实质上禁止多妻 |

古兰经 4:129 的完整译文（注释 13）：

> "Ye are never able to be fair and just as between wives, even if it is your ardent desire." (p. 179)

### 法学分析

**核心原则**：禁止监护人娶其照管下的孤女，因为存在**利益冲突**（conflict of interest）——监护人应为被监护人争取利益，若自己娶她则无法履行此职责。(p. 171)

**针对美貌孤女**：监护人一般被禁止娶她们，除非能公正对待、支付完整聘礼；最好将其嫁给他人以确保社会监督。(p. 171)

**针对富裕孤女**：监护人可能觊觎其财产，而婚姻能改变孤女的法律地位——她作为妻子获得丈夫赡养费和遗产的合法权利，同时巩固对自有财产的权利。(p. 171–172)

**方法论意义**：阿伊莎——一位女性历史人物——为当代穆斯林学者树立了以经解经的先例，并以此倡导最弱势群体的权利。

> "This precedent is embodied in the historical figure of a woman, 'Aisha, who advocated for the rights of orphan girls. This is a powerful source of inspiration and authority for contemporary Muslims who similarly seek to protect the legal rights of the most disempowered members of society." (p. 172)

## 五、结论性观点

### 1. 圣训文本解释优于历史考据

Chaudhry 认为参与圣训**文本解释**比对圣训的源头批评或历史分析更有利于法律变革：

> "Engaging with prophetic reports through interpretation of their texts is more effective for legal change than source criticism or historical analysis of prophetic reports. This is because historical criticism of prophetic reports is largely an academic enterprise with little bearing on the meaning-generating power of *ahadith* for living Muslim communities." (p. 174)

### 2. 圣训的规范性内容与具体内容的区分

尽管圣训的具体内容可能在伦理上存在问题（如未成年人婚姻），但其**规范性内容**提供了有价值的指导：

> "Although the specific content of the reports may be ethically problematic and unsuitable for a modern, gender-egalitarian Islamic law (e.g. the marriage of minors), the normative content of the prophetic reports offers valuable guidance that is applicable and relevant to pre- and postcolonial Muslim concerns." (p. 174)

两条核心规范信息：
- **女性不得被违背意愿嫁出**
- **掌权者不得利用其照管下的弱势者**

> "The normative messages are that women cannot be married against their will and that those in power should not take advantage of those in their care." (p. 174)

### 3. 承认父权与反父权并存

> "It is useful to acknowledge openly the patriarchal nature of prophetic reports alongside the assertion that these reports also contain counter-patriarchal influences. This approach captures the complex nature of prophetic reports and resists categorizing Prophet Muhammad's behaviour in a simplistic manner." (p. 173)

### 4. 社区解释权的回归

> "By shifting the power of *hadith* interpretation to living communities, those communities can take responsibility and be held accountable for their interpretive choices." (p. 174)

## 六、教法学推导方法总结

| 方法 | 内容 | 法律意义 |
|---|---|---|
| 文本（matn）优先于传述链（isnad） | 关注圣训正文的规范内容，不以传述链强弱为过滤标准 | 扩大可用圣训范围，符合前殖民时期法学实践 |
| 选择性引用的合法化 | 援引前殖民经注学家/法学家选择性引用的先例 | 性别平等导向的选择性阅读在方法论上并无新意 |
| 以经解经（*tafsir al-Qur'an bi-l-Qur'an*） | 用不同古兰经经文互释同一经文 | 互文选择决定法律结论（如 4:3 究竟关于孤女权利还是多妻制） |
| "典范"与"例外"区分 | 区分先知行为中可效仿的与属于特殊情况的部分 | 允许搁置而非否定伦理上有争议的先知行为 |
| 规范内容与具体内容的分离 | 提取圣训的普遍原则而非拘泥于具体规定 | 为超越字面内容的法律改革提供宗教依据 |
