# 穆斯林法律传统与性别平等的挑战

**论文信息：** Ziba Mir-Hosseini, "Muslim Legal Tradition and the Challenge of Gender Equality"

---

## 一、核心论题

本文追溯了二十世纪性别平等观念如何对穆斯林法律传统构成挑战。作者聚焦三位改革思想家在家庭法改革关键时刻发表的文本，论证当前歧视性法律既非神圣、亦非不可更改，而是建立在一个不再有效的教法学假设之上的法学建构。

---

## 二、男性权威作为法律公设（Qiwamah Postulate）

### 2.1 经文基础与教法学推导

古典法学家从《古兰经》4:34 推导出「卡瓦玛」（qiwamah）概念，将其发展为定义和规范性别关系的指导原则。

> "Men are *qawwamun* [protectors/maintainers] in relation to women, according to what God has favored some over others and according to what they spend from their wealth. Righteous women are *qanitat* [obedient], guarding the unseen according to what God has guarded. Those [women] whose *nushuz* [rebellion] you fear, admonish them, and abandon them in bed, and *adribuhunna* [strike them]."
>
> —— p. 38

**译文：** 男人是女人的「卡瓦蒙」（保护者/供养者），这是基于真主使一些人优于另一些人，以及他们从财产中的支出。贤淑的女人是「卡尼塔特」（顺从的），守护真主所守护的隐秘。你们担忧其「努舒兹」（叛逆）的女人，则劝诫她们，在床上疏远她们，（最后）「阿德里布胡纳」（打她们）。

作者引用 Kecia Ali 的翻译方式，指出这些关键术语的每一种翻译本身就构成一种解释（p. 38）。

### 2.2 卡瓦玛公设的运作机制

Lynn Welchman 将此称为「卡瓦玛公设」（qiwamah postulate）：

> "using 'postulate' in the sense defined by Masaji Chiba (1986, p. 7): 'A value system that simply exists in its own right'. It operates in all areas of Muslim law relating to gender rights, but its impact is most evident in the laws that classical jurists devised for the regulation of marriage and divorce."
>
> —— p. 39

**译文：** 使用 Chiba 所定义的"公设"——"一种自在自洽的价值体系"。它运作于穆斯林法律中与性别权利相关的所有领域，但在古典法学家为规范婚姻和离婚所制定的法律中影响最为显著。

### 2.3 婚姻的法律结构

古典法学家将婚姻定义为一种交换合同，以买卖合同（bay'）为模型：

> "The contract, called *'aqd al-nikah* (the contract of coitus), has three essential elements: *ijab*, the offer made by the woman or her guardian; *qubul*, acceptance by the husband; and *mahr*, a gift from the husband to the person of the bride."
>
> —— p. 39

**译文：** 该合同称为「尼卡赫之约」（性交之约），包含三个基本要素：伊贾卜（要约），由女方或其监护人提出；古布勒（承诺），由丈夫接受；麦赫尔（聘礼），丈夫赠予新娘本人的礼物。

### 2.4 核心法律概念：塔姆金与纳法卡

合同确立了围绕**性接触与补偿**两大主题的权利义务：

> "*Tamkin*, obedience or submission, specifically sexual access, becomes the husband's right and thus the wife's duty; whereas *nafaqah*, maintenance, specifically shelter, food and clothing, becomes the wife's right and the husband's duty. But if a wife is in a state of *nushuz* (disobedience) she loses her claim to maintenance."
>
> —— p. 39

**译文：** 塔姆金（顺从），即性接触权，成为丈夫的权利，因而是妻子的义务；而纳法卡（供养），即住所、食物和衣物，成为妻子的权利和丈夫的义务。但若妻子处于「努舒兹」（不服从）状态，她将失去供养请求权。

### 2.5 离婚权的不对称

> "Whereas the husband has the unilateral and extra-judicial right to terminate the contract by *talaq* or repudiation, a wife can only terminate the contract with her husband's consent or the intervention of the court – if she produces a valid reason."
>
> —— pp. 39–40

**译文：** 丈夫拥有通过「塔拉格」（休妻）单方面、无需经过司法的终止合同权利，而妻子只能在丈夫同意或法院介入的情况下终止合同——且须提供正当理由。

### 2.6 供养与服从的逻辑

法学家们在供养的性质上存在分歧：

> "They disagreed, Ibn Rushd (1996) tells us, over 'whether maintenance is a counter-value for (sexual) utilization, or compensation for the fact that she is confined because of her husband, as in the case of one absent or sick' (p. 63). And it was within the parameters of this logic – men provide and women obey – that notions of gender rights and justice acquired their meanings."
>
> —— p. 40

**译文：** 伊本·鲁世德告诉我们，他们争论的是"供养是对（性）使用的对价，还是对她因丈夫而被限制（行动）这一事实的补偿，如丈夫外出或生病的情况"。正是在"男人供养、女人服从"这一逻辑框架内，性别权利和正义的观念获得了其含义。

### 2.7 卡瓦玛作为其他法律不平等的理论根基

> "I suggest that the juristic construct of *qiwamah*, developed in the context of the marriage contract, provided the rationale for other legal disparities, such as men's rights to polygamy and to unilateral repudiation, women's lesser share in inheritance, and the ban on women being judges or political leaders."
>
> —— p. 41

**译文：** 我认为，在婚姻合同语境中发展出的教法学建构——卡瓦玛——为其他法律差异提供了合理化依据，如男性的多妻权、单方休妻权、女性较少的遗产份额，以及禁止女性担任法官或政治领袖。

## 三、三位改革思想家的教法学方法论

### 3.1 第一阶段：法典化时期——塔希尔·哈达德（Tahir al-Haddad, 1899–1936）

#### 3.1.1 区分永恒原则与权变规范

> "We should take into consideration the great difference between what Islam brought and its aims, which will remain immortal in eternity, such as belief in monotheism, moral behaviour, and the establishment of justice, dignity and equality among people. Furthermore, we have to consider the social situation and the deep-rooted mind set that existed in Arab society in the pre-Islamic era when Islam first emerged."
>
> —— p. 45–46 (引自 Husni and Newman, 2007, p. 36)

**译文：** 我们应当考虑伊斯兰所带来的内容及其目标之间的巨大差异——后者将永恒不朽，如对独一神的信仰、道德行为、以及在人们之间建立正义、尊严和平等。此外，我们必须考虑伊斯兰初兴时阿拉伯社会中存在的社会状况和根深蒂固的思维方式。

#### 3.1.2 渐进主义政策（al-siyasa al-tadrijiyya）

哈达德提出「渐进立法政策」，认为《古兰经》和圣训中的立法过程是循序渐进的：

> "In Islam the 'highest aim is equality among all God's creatures', but it was not possible to achieve this aim in the seventh century and during the lifetime of the Prophet… 'Islam is the religion of freedom', but it tolerated 'the selling and buying of human beings as goods, and their exploitation as animals for the duration of their lives'."
>
> —— p. 46 (引自 Husni and Newman, 2007, pp. 48, 104)

**译文：** 在伊斯兰中，"最高目标是真主所有造物间的平等"，但在七世纪和先知在世时不可能实现这一目标……"伊斯兰是自由的宗教"，但它容忍了"将人类作为商品买卖，并在其一生中像牲畜一样剥削他们"。

哈达德以奴隶制类比父权制：正如《古兰经》鼓励释放奴隶、确立自由原则，它同样容忍了父权制但确立了平等原则。

#### 3.1.3 重新解读4:34和2:228

哈达德认为两节经文必须在当时的婚姻和离婚习俗背景下阅读：

> "This verse is not speaking about the rights and duties of spouses, but is about the course of action to be taken when there is marital discord, and it offers ways to resolve such discord."
>
> —— p. 47

**译文：** 这节经文谈论的不是配偶的权利和义务，而是关于出现婚姻不和时应采取的行动方针，并提供了解决此类不和的途径。

#### 3.1.4 从交易逻辑转向情感基础

哈达德将婚姻的教法学焦点从4:34转移到30:21：

> "Marriage involves affection, duties, intercourse and procreation. Islam regards affection as the foundation of marriage since it is the driving force."
>
> —— p. 48 (引自 Husni and Newman, 2007, p. 57)

**译文：** 婚姻涉及感情、责任、交合和生育。伊斯兰将感情视为婚姻的基础，因为它是驱动力。

> "By shifting the focus from verse 4:34 to verse 30:21, al-Haddad was able to break the link not only between maintenance and obedience as constructed in classical fiqh texts, but also between male authority over women (*qiwamah*), as derived from verse 4:34, and male superiority (*darajah*), derived from verse 2:228."
>
> —— pp. 48–49

**译文：** 通过将焦点从4:34转移到30:21，哈达德不仅打破了古典教法学文本中所建构的供养与服从之间的联系，而且打破了源于4:34的男性对女性的权威（卡瓦玛）与源于2:228的男性优越性（达拉贾）之间的联系。

#### 3.1.5 法律实践影响

1956年突尼斯独立后，在布尔吉巴领导下将哈达德的许多改革建议纳入法典：

> "The new code made polygamy illegal and gave women equal access to divorce and child custody, though the inheritance laws remained unchanged."
>
> —— p. 49

**译文：** 新法典将多妻制定为违法，赋予妇女平等的离婚和子女监护权，但遗产法保持不变。

---

### 3.2 第二阶段：政治伊斯兰崛起——法兹卢尔·拉赫曼（Fazlur Rahman, 1919–1988）

#### 3.2.1 《古兰经》非法典

> "The Qur'an, Rahman contends, is not a book of law; it 'is the divine response, through the Prophet's mind, to the moral-social situation of the Prophet's Arabia, particularly to the problems of commercial Meccan society of the day'."
>
> —— p. 52 (引自 Rahman, 1982a, p. 5)

**译文：** 拉赫曼主张，《古兰经》不是一部法律书籍；它是"通过先知的心灵，对先知的阿拉伯所面临的道德-社会境况的神圣回应，特别是对当时麦加商业社会问题的回应"。

#### 3.2.2 本质与偶然的区分

> "One must completely accept our general contention that the specific legal rules of the Qur'an are conditioned by the sociohistorical background of their enactment and what is eternal therein is the social objectives or moral principles explicitly stated or strongly implied in that legislation."
>
> —— p. 56 (引自 Rahman, 1982b, p. 301)

**译文：** 人们必须完全接受我们的一般论点：《古兰经》中具体的法律规则受其颁布时的社会历史背景所制约，其中永恒的是该立法中明确陈述或强烈暗示的社会目标或道德原则。

#### 3.2.3 对4:34的解读

拉赫曼认为4:34给出了男性优越性的两个理据，均与社会经济因素有关：

> "Verse 4:34 'gives two rationales for male superiority: (1) that man is "more excellent", and (2) that man is charged entirely with household expenditure'."
>
> —— p. 54 (引自 Rahman, 1982b, p. 294)

**译文：** 4:34节"为男性优越性给出了两个理据：（1）男人'更优秀'，（2）男人完全承担家庭开支"。

#### 3.2.4 社会经济角色的可变性

> "These inequalities are not inherent in the nature of the sexes; they are the product of historical socioeconomic developments. Once women acquire education and participate in society and economy, the 'degree' that the Qur'an says men have over women also disappears."
>
> —— p. 55

**译文：** 这些不平等并非内在于两性本质；它们是历史性社会经济发展的产物。一旦女性获得教育并参与社会和经济，《古兰经》所说的男性对女性的那个"等级"也会消失。

#### 3.2.5 法律改革的必要性

> "classical *fiqh* rulings in marriage, divorce and inheritance can and must be reformed because 'it is the most fundamental and urgent requirement of the Qur'an in the social sector that abuses and injustices be removed'."
>
> —— p. 55 (引自 Rahman, 1982b, p. 295)

**译文：** 婚姻、离婚和遗产方面的古典教法学裁决能够而且必须改革，因为"消除虐待和不公是《古兰经》在社会领域最根本和最紧迫的要求"。

### 3.3 第三阶段：伊斯兰女权主义出现——纳斯尔·阿布·扎伊德（Nasr Abu Zayd, 1943–2010）

#### 3.3.1 教法学与《古兰经》婚姻观的鸿沟

阿布·扎伊德引用穆罕默德·阿卜杜赫的对比：

> "Marriage, according to *fiqh*, is a contract which renders the female vagina the property of a male. The Qur'an's view, however, is that marriage is one of the Divine signs (*ayat*)."
>
> —— p. 60 (引自 Abu Zayd, 2013, p. 153)

**译文：** 根据教法学，婚姻是一份使女性阴道成为男性财产的合同。然而，《古兰经》的观点是，婚姻是神圣迹象（阿亚特）之一。

#### 3.3.2 伊斯兰学科的碎片化

> "The worlds of the Qur'an, or its multi-dimensional worldview, were separated, in fact fragmented. Theology took over the world of divinity… philosophy took over the world of metaphysics… Sufism took over the ethical-spiritual world; and legal theory took over the legislative world."
>
> —— p. 61 (引自 Abu Zayd, 2013, p. 154)

**译文：** 《古兰经》的世界，或其多维世界观，被分离，实际上被碎片化了。神学接管了神性世界……哲学接管了形而上学世界……苏菲主义接管了伦理-精神世界；而法学理论接管了立法世界。

阿布·扎伊德指出，在《古兰经》6,236节经文中，至多只有500节包含法律内容（p. 61）。

#### 3.3.3 五个意义域框架

阿布·扎伊德提出将《古兰经》的多维世界观分为五个相互关联的域：1）宇宙论；2）神-人关系；3）伦理道德维度；4）社会层面；5）刑罚（hudud）（p. 63）。

#### 3.3.4 宇宙论与伦理层面的平等

> "As the chapter about women opens with absolute cosmological equality, the entire chapter, which contains most of the legal regulations concerning marriage, should always be connected to the principle of equality."
>
> —— p. 64 (引自 Abu Zayd, 2013, p. 161)

**译文：** 既然关于妇女的章节以绝对的宇宙论平等开篇，那么包含大多数婚姻法律规定的整个章节，都应始终与平等原则相联系。

#### 3.3.5 社会层面的分化非歧视

> "Qur'anic gender differentiation developed into discrimination in the *fiqh* literature due to a certain cultural and socio-historical context."
>
> —— p. 65 (引自 Abu Zayd, 2013, p. 162)

**译文：** 《古兰经》的性别分化在教法学文献中发展为歧视，这是由于特定的文化和社会历史语境。

#### 3.3.6 核心追问

> "[W]hy should we demand of the Qur'an that it violate the established rules in the societal domain of meaning? It should be recognised that when the Qur'an sustains absolute equality in both the cosmological and the ethical–spiritual domain, this is the direction in which the Qur'an would like Muslims to upgrade the societal domain of inequality."
>
> —— p. 65 (引自 Abu Zayd, 2013, p. 164)

**译文：** 我们为什么要要求《古兰经》违反社会意义域中的既定规则？应该认识到，当《古兰经》在宇宙论和伦理-精神域维持绝对平等时，这正是《古兰经》希望穆斯林提升社会不平等域的方向。

## 四、法律实践：法典化的历史进程

### 4.1 二十世纪的家庭法改革

> "During the twentieth century, many Muslim-majority countries adopted new legal codes relating to marriage and family. The new codes were based on classical *fiqh* rulings, but incorporated reformed elements in order to accommodate some modern expectations and realities."
>
> —— p. 43

**译文：** 二十世纪期间，许多穆斯林占多数的国家采纳了有关婚姻和家庭的新法典。这些新法典以古典教法学裁决为基础，但纳入了改革元素以适应一些现代期望和现实。

改革手段包括：混合不同教法学派的原则和裁决（takhayyur/talfiq），以及通过程序性手段，但并未直接挑战婚姻的父权建构（pp. 43–44）。

### 4.2 改革的矛盾效果

> "While making some concessions to contemporary demands for improved rights for women, the effect of these reforms was not only to give the state an unprecedented authority to enforce its chosen prescriptions for marriage procedures and family practices, but also to entrench traditional patriarchal interpretations of the sacred texts and the legal and ethical assumptions behind them."
>
> —— p. 44

**译文：** 虽然这些改革在一定程度上回应了改善妇女权利的当代要求，但其效果不仅赋予国家前所未有的权力来强制执行其选定的婚姻程序和家庭实践规定，而且进一步巩固了对神圣文本的传统父权解释及其背后的法律和伦理假设。

### 4.3 政治伊斯兰对改革的逆转

> "The rise of Islam as both a spiritual and a political force in the 1970s brought a reversal of the movement towards secularization of laws and legal systems."
>
> —— p. 50

**译文：** 1970年代伊斯兰作为精神和政治力量的崛起，带来了法律和法律体系世俗化运动的逆转。

## 五、平等家庭法的前景

### 5.1 卡瓦玛与威拉亚是"父权的DNA"

> "By establishing gender hierarchy and discrimination, the juristic concepts *qiwamah* and *wilayah* are in effect the 'DNA of patriarchy' in Muslim legal tradition. Those who seek to establish an egalitarian construction of gender rights in Muslim contexts must address and redefine the understanding of these legal concepts in line with contemporary notions of justice."
>
> —— p. 66

**译文：** 通过建立性别等级制度和歧视，教法学概念卡瓦玛和威拉亚实际上是穆斯林法律传统中"父权的DNA"。寻求在穆斯林语境中建立平等性别权利建构的人，必须按照当代正义观念重新定义对这些法律概念的理解。

### 5.2 正义观念的变迁

穆斯林法律思想传统上依据亚里士多德式的应得正义观运作：

> "'justice is maintaining everything in its proper place': men and women have their proper, essential places in family and society, and justice consists of keeping them in these places and giving them rights accordingly."
>
> —— p. 68

**译文：** "正义就是维持万物在其适当位置"：男人和女人在家庭和社会中有其适当的、本质性的位置，正义在于让他们留在这些位置并据此赋予他们权利。

### 5.3 改革策略总结

> "Chief among these strategies have been differentiating the changeable from the unchangeable in the texts… discerning the Lawgiver's aims (*maqasid*) and the changes that these aims would have led to in the course of time; and locating both the sacred texts and the rulings that the classical *fuqaha* derived from them in their historical and political contexts."
>
> —— pp. 68–69

**译文：** 这些策略中最主要的是：区分文本中可变与不可变的内容……辨识立法者的目的（麦嘎绥德）以及这些目的在历史进程中本应导致的变化；将神圣文本及古典法学家从中推导出的裁决都置于其历史和政治语境中。

### 5.4 问题不在文本，而在语境

> "the problem is not with the text but with context and the ways in which the text is used to sustain patriarchal and authoritarian structures."
>
> —— p. 70

**译文：** 问题不在于文本本身，而在于语境以及文本被用来维持父权和威权结构的方式。
