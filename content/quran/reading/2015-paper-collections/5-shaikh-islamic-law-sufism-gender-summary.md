# Sa'diyya Shaikh: Islamic Law, Sufism and Gender — Rethinking the Terms of the Debate

## 基本信息

- **作者**: Sa'diyya Shaikh
- **出处**: *Men in Charge? Rethinking Authority in Muslim Legal Tradition*, ed. Ziba Mir-Hosseini et al., London: Oneworld, 2015
- **页码范围**: 181–219
- **关键词**: 苏菲主义（Sufism）、教法学（fiqh）、宗教人类学（religious anthropology）、伊本·阿拉比（Ibn Arabi）、完人（al-Insan al-Kamil）、威严与美（jalal/jamal）、基瓦玛（qiwamah）、维拉亚（wilayah）

## 一、核心论点

Shaikh 主张：特定苏菲视角可以为重新思考伊斯兰法中的性别伦理提供深层的**本体论和形而上学基础**。传统教法学（fiqh）的性别等级观念植根于有缺陷的**"宗教人类学"**——即对"人之为人"的宗教理解。通过引入苏菲神学概念，可以批判并重建教法学所依赖的性别假设。

> "Traditional Muslim personal law is constrained by its own underlying notions of human nature. From a contemporary Islamic feminist perspective, the limited gender understandings of human nature, as developed in different sociohistorical contexts, serve as central deficits in various iterations of classical Islamic law." (p. 181)

> "I suggest that particular Sufi discourses present substantial resources for more relevant, enriching and benevolent interpretations of the *Shari'ah* and the related understandings of human nature reflected in the Qur'an than do the prevailing *fiqh* discourses." (p. 182)

**Shaikh 的关键区分**：苏菲传统并非性别问题上的乌托邦——它同样受到其形成时期的父权制社会环境的影响，但其核心原则中蕴含着深刻的**性别包容性**资源。

> "Let me be clear: Sufis do not hold a singular position on gender nor is Sufism an ahistorical panacea of all things good and benevolent for women." (p. 184)

## 二、方法论框架：超越权利话语的结构性批判

### 1. 教法学（fiqh）与沙里亚（Shari'ah）的区分

Shaikh 强调教法学是人类对沙里亚的解释性尝试，而非沙里亚本身。沙里亚指向一种道德、宗教和愿景性的人类实现之路，教法学则是将这一愿景转化为法律规范的多元人类诠释过程。

> "The term *Shari'ah* signifies such a vision of a moral, religious, visionary path for human fulfilment – it does not signify law in the ways we recognize Muslim law. Rather, *fiqh* represents multiple and varied human interpretive attempts to translate this vision into legal norms." (p. 191)

将沙里亚与教法学混为一谈具有意识形态后果，尤其在当今政治话语中：

> "While for most legal scholars it is a theoretical commonplace that the *fiqh* canon represents limited human attempts to express the *Shari'ah* and that *fiqh* is the product of dynamic human processes, more ideologized and simplistic conflations of *Shari'ah* and *fiqh* often appear in popular political discourse. This phenomenon continues to have detrimental consequences for gender justice and women's rights in many Muslim societies." (p. 193)

### 2. 权利话语的局限性

Shaikh 肯定权利话语在策略和实践上的必要性，但指出其不足：权利话语处理的是父权制的**症状**，而非**根源**。

> "A potential shortcoming of a discourse focused solely on rights, however, is that it attends to the symptomatic effects of patriarchy without necessarily providing a thorough structural critique." (p. 189)

> "A pure rights-based discourse is limited by the fact that it often deals with the symptoms of inherited structures of patriarchal discourse without necessarily or rigorously interrogating the nature, roots and assumptions of the structures – in other words, the causal factors informing such structures." (p. 190)

权利话语的另一个风险是：在穆斯林身份受到威胁的地缘政治环境中，容易退缩回父权制教法学：

> "In the current geopolitical environment, where Muslim identity in different contexts may be perceived as under threat, reversions to patriarchal *fiqh* as the benchmark of Muslim identity and authenticity become particularly salient and charged." (p. 190)

### 3. 结构性批判的必要性

Shaikh 提出需要从**神学层面**审视教法学的基本前提：

> "Developing a more structural and theologically robust critique requires critical attention to the constructed nature of *fiqh* as a human and historically evolving interpretation of *Shari'ah*." (p. 190)

核心问题包括：教法学文献中"人"、"社会"和"真主"等概念的实质内容是什么？继承下来的教法学是否真正体现了对终极现实和人类目的的最佳当代理解？

> "What is the notional content of the terms 'human being', 'society' and 'God' which underlies dominant positions in the *fiqh* literature? Most significantly, it is imperative to look at the nature and constitution of *fiqh* in relation to a deeper vision of ultimate reality and human purpose." (p. 191)

### 4. 权利话语学者的代表性立场

| 学者 | 方法 | 局限 |
|---|---|---|
| **Quraishi (2002)** | 传统教法学赋予女性的权利比社会实际给予的更多 | 仍在现有框架内运作 |
| **Sonbol (1996)** | 前现代教法学的多元性曾给予穆斯林女性更多自由 | 回溯性辩护 |
| **al-Hibri (2000)** | 优先选择对女性有财务优势的传统法律立场（如赡养费、聘礼） | 竞争性平等话语 |

> "The goal, then, is to retrieve rights for women that have been marginalized in dominant interpretations of the Qur'an and *Shari'ah*. This effectively translates into a discourse of competing equalities." (p. 189–190)

## 三、苏菲心理学中的自我与主体性

### 1. 灵魂（nafs）的三种状态

Shaikh 援引苏菲传统中人格的三元结构：灵魂（nafs）、心灵（qalb）和精神（ruh），其中灵魂是一个动态实体，随个人灵性状态而变化。

**(a) 命令之灵魂（al-nafs al-ammara）**：最粗糙、最未精炼的状态，"煽动作恶的灵魂"（古兰经 12:53），被自我中心和利己倾向主宰。

> "Dominated by self-centred and egoistic tendencies, it draws a person to the realm of limited selfhood and transient desires and is responsible for separation from the original state of harmony between God and humanity." (p. 194)

**(b) 责备之灵魂（al-nafs al-lawwama）**：良知的觉醒，灵魂意识到自身缺陷，对任何灵性疏忽进行自我责备（古兰经 75:2）。

> "Such transformations mark the emergence of the conscience, where the striving for good has been integrated and internalized." (p. 195)

**(c) 安宁之灵魂（al-nafs al-mutmainna）**：通过持续的灵性净化，心灵被神圣之光照亮，灵魂进入安宁状态（古兰经 89:27）。

> "With consistent striving and purification, the heart is cleansed and illuminated by the divine light of the spirit, and the soul of the seeker is satisfied." (p. 195)

### 2. 苏菲心理学对父权制的固有批判

苏菲心理学的核心——对真主绝对主权的持续觉知——天然地反对人类对权力的声索，包括男性对女性的权威声索：

> "The commitment to a constant awareness of God's absolute sovereignty counters the human instinct to claim power, including male claims to authority over women. In this framework, any such claim demands interrogation and may be suspected as a potential trap of the lower self (*al-nafs al-ammara*)." (p. 196)

### 3. 拉比亚的叙事——苏菲传统中的性别批判

Shaikh 援引早期苏菲大师拉比亚·阿达维亚（卒于 801 年）的著名故事：一群宗教男性试图挑衅她，声称一切美德、先知之冠和高贵之带都属于男性。拉比亚回答：

> "'All that is true, but egoism and self-worship and "I am your Lord" have never sprung from a woman's breast…All these things have been the specialty of men.'" (Attar, 1966, p. 48; 引于 p. 196)

**法律与神学意义**：拉比亚将男性对基于性别的优越性的声索与古兰经中不信仰的原型——法老——联系起来（法老的话"我是你们的主"出自古兰经 79:24），将父权制权力欲望等同于最深层的灵性疾病。

> "This pithy story, with its radical set of Qur'anic associations, condemns men's desire for privilege as symptomatic of a severe spiritual deficit. Rabia astutely points to the triumph of *al-nafs al-ammara* over men through chauvinism." (p. 197)

> "Developing this analysis allows us to infer that patriarchy is reflective of a lack of spiritual discernment." (p. 197)

### 4. 苏菲传统中的厌女元素

Shaikh 承认苏菲文学中存在厌女倾向——一些苏菲男性将女性与命令之灵魂的破坏性吸引力联系在一起：

> "Some Sufi men linked the dangers of an overwhelming sexuality to women, relegating both to the realm of *al-nafs al-ammara*. However, their wariness towards women clearly represented an outward projection of the inner struggles that these men were having with their own desires." (p. 197–198)

## 四、伊本·阿拉比的本体论框架

### 1. 神圣名称与完人（al-Insan al-Kamil）

伊本·阿拉比（卒于 1240 年）——一位同时具有苏菲和法学家身份的非凡思想家——基于圣训"真主按自己的形象创造了亚当"和古兰经 2:30"真主教授了亚当所有的名称"，建立了他的宗教人类学：

> "The 'names' signify the attributes or qualities of God, what the Qur'an also describes as 'the beautiful names' (*al-asma al-husna*). Traditionally, it is held that God has ninety-nine names, qualities or attributes that reside within God's state of unity (*tawhid*)." (p. 198)

人类是唯一能够综合体现真主所有属性的造物——即神圣名称的微观宇宙：

> "Ibn Arabi states that among all creation humanity uniquely reflects the potential to comprehensively integrate and manifest the totality of God's attributes; in other words, a microcosm of the divine names." (p. 198–199)

"完人"（al-Insan al-Kamil）代表了理想的伦理自我和人类的模范标准：

> "Ibn Arabi presents a particular religious anthropology through developing the Sufi archetypal human, called the *al-Insan al-Kamil* ('the Complete Human'), which represents the ideal ethical self and the exemplary standard for human beings." (p. 199)

### 2. 威严（jalal）与美（jamal）的辩证关系

神圣名称分为两大类：

| 类别 | 名称举例 | 与造物的关系 |
|---|---|---|
| **美的名称（jamal）** | 仁爱的、慈悲的、恩惠的、温和的、宽恕的 | 与真主同造物的**相似性**相连 |
| **威严的名称（jalal）** | 不可接近的、致死的、压倒性的、至高的、伟大的 | 与真主的**超越性**相连 |

**核心原则**：在灵性修行中，美的属性（jamal）具有认识论和本体论上的优先性。真主在神圣圣训（hadith qudsi）中说："我的慈悯先于我的怒火。"

> "This epistemological priority accorded to the *jamali* attributes for the seeker is linked to its larger ontological priority within the being of God, who says in a *hadith qudsi*, 'My mercy precedes my wrath.'" (p. 201)

**对抗错置的威严**：撒旦在被命令向亚当叩头时回答"我比他好"，体现了错置的威严（jalal），因此被逐出与真主亲近的领域：

> "Satan epitomized misplaced *jalal* when he countered God's command to prostrate himself before Adam with the claim that 'I am better than he', reflecting an arrogance born of a misplaced sense of power and majesty." (p. 200)

### 3. 从本体论到社会伦理

灵性发展要求一种社会参与的关怀伦理——通过在人际关系中体现爱、慈悯、同情和温柔来精炼品格：

> "One's spiritual transformation occurs significantly through embodying certain types of behaviour in relation to other people. Character is refined through cultivating social interactions based on love, mercy, compassion and gentleness towards our fellow beings." (p. 201)

**对教法学的启示**：以慈悯的本体论命令为基础的法律体系必须深刻地致力于对男女一视同仁的关怀与正义伦理：

> "Informed by ontological imperatives of mercy, a legal system would need to reflect a deep commitment to an ethics of care and justice for men and women alike. In contemporary times, it is clear that traditional Muslim laws seldom meet such conceptions of care or justice." (p. 203)

**对父权制权力关系的批判**：通过突出人性中的美（jamal）面向，伊本·阿拉比的框架不仅批判社会等级和歧视意识形态，还拒绝崇尚侵略性和未精炼的威严（jalal）品质的社会结构：

> "By foregrounding the *jamali* aspects of humanity, this approach not only provides a general critique of social hierarchies and discriminatory ideologies; it also rejects social structures that prize aggression and other unrefined *jalali* qualities." (p. 202)

## 五、伊本·阿拉比论性别与法律

### 1. 灵性平等的明确肯定

伊本·阿拉比反复声明"完人"的标准是**无性别的**，对男女提出相同要求，且男女均可同等达到：

> "Ibn Arabi repeatedly says that *al-Insan al-Kamil*, the standard for spiritual completion, is ungendered, makes identical demands on men and women and is attainable equally by both." (p. 203)

> "'There is no spiritual qualification conferred on men which is denied women.'" (Ibn Arabi, 1911, vol. 2, p. 35; 引于 p. 203)

他甚至明确肯定女性有能力达到任何时代最高的灵性品位——轴心圣人（qutb）。(p. 203)

### 2. 灵性能力直接转化为社会和法律领域

**这是伊本·阿拉比框架中对教法学最直接的意涵**——灵性平等必然导向社会和法律领域的平等：

> "Particularly significant for our purposes, Ibn Arabi clearly connects women's equal spiritual capacity to agency in the social realm and, specifically, in the law." (p. 204)

### 3. 案例一：女性设立法律先例的能力

伊本·阿拉比援引哈哲尔（Hajar）的案例——她是朝觐中赛义（sa'i）仪式的创始者，因此是一项**适用于全体穆斯林社区的法律先例的创立者**：

> "He invokes the case of Hajar as the initiator of the *sa'i* rites during hajj, making her the creator of a legal precedent that is applicable to the entire Muslim community." (p. 204)

这种社会法律能力源于女性灵性完美的潜力——灵性能力与设立社区法律先例的能力之间存在明确的性别联系：

> "This sociolegal capacity, he argues, emerges only as a consequence of women's potential for spiritual perfection. The gendered link between spiritual capacity and the ability to set communal legal precedents reflects an explicit connection between spirituality and the law in Ibn Arabi's framework." (p. 204–205)

### 4. 案例二：女性法律证词的效力

伊本·阿拉比引用了一个在其他法学家中并非不常见的观点——尽管传统上对女性法律证词有限制，但存在一名女性的证词等于两名男性证词的情况：

> "Usually a judge does not make a definite judgement except with the testimony of two men. Yet in some circumstances the testimony of one woman equals that of two men. For example, the judge's acceptance of her testimony about menstrual cycles as it related to the waiting period after divorce (*'iddah*), or the husband accepting her statement about his paternity of the child." (Ibn Arabi, 1911, vol. 3, p. 89; 引于 p. 205)

**法学推导**：证词的效力取决于**经验和知识基础**，而非性别的内在优劣。在伊本·阿拉比的时代背景下，女性的经验主要限于身体和私人领域，男性活跃于商业公共领域——因此证词权重与各自的经验领域相关：

> "Here Ibn Arabi is alerting us that context and experience are principal considerations when determining gender-specific legal capacity. Such an approach suggests that legal rulings appearing to favour men may simply be responsive to the realities and pragmatics of the social arena." (p. 205)

**当代意涵**：鉴于性别关系发生了巨大的社会和历史变化，这一方法要求法律体系对人类性别化经验的变迁做出回应：

> "Given the vast social and historical changes in gender relations, this approach demands a legal system that is responsive to the shifts in human gendered experiences." (p. 206)

### 5. 案例三：遮羞（'awrah）与衣着

伊本·阿拉比的立场建立在性别平等的宗教人类学之上。他援引古兰经中亚当与夏娃的叙事（二人被命令遮盖羞体），推断**男女的遮羞范围相同**——仅限于生殖器：

> "'In our opinion, the only parts of her body that are *'awrah* are her genitals. God, the Exalted, says: "When they tasted of the tree, their shameful parts became manifest to them, and they began to sew together the leaves of the Garden over their bodies." God put Adam and Eve on equal footing regarding the covering of their shameful parts, which are their genitals. If women are still ordered to cover their bodies, it is for the sake of modesty, and not because their bodies are shameful.'" (Ibn Arabi, 1911, vol. 1, p. 408; 引于 p. 206–207)

**法学推导**：

1. **本体论层面**：女性身体在本体论和内在性质上**并不**比男性身体需要更多遮盖——伊本·阿拉比驳斥了"女性身体在本质上是羞耻的"这一普遍观念。
2. **社会层面**：超出生殖器遮盖的端庄要求是**社会生成的**而非本体论驱动的——"如果女性仍然被命令遮盖身体的其余部分，这是为了端庄（modesty），而不是因为她们的身体是可耻的"。
3. **条件性措辞**：他使用条件词"如果"（if）开始关于端庄命令的陈述，暗示社会偶然性。

> "The element of social contingency is also reflected in the conditional 'if' with which he begins this statement regarding the command for modesty." (p. 207)

### 6. 案例四：女性先知与使者

伊本·阿拉比援引圣训——麦尔彦（Maryam）和阿西娅（Asiya）均达到了灵性完美（kamal），即先知品位（nubuwwa）。这在当时是少数派观点——多数学者认为女性先知的观念是异端：

> "Ibn Arabi attributes prophecy to women in the cases of Maryam and Asiya even though the consensus among his contemporaries regarded ideas of female prophecy as heresy." (p. 209)

**关于"最高完美"（akmaliyya）的限定**：男女在完美（kamal）层面相会；男性仅在"最高完美"上具有优先性——即使者（risala）和先知使命（batha）的品位专属于男性：

> "'Men and women come together at the level [*darajah*] of perfection [*kamal*]. And men are given priority with superlative perfection [*akmaliyya*], not with perfection.'" (Ibn Arabi, 1911, vol. 3, p. 87; 引于 p. 208)

**关键的神学论证**：先知穆罕默德封印了先知品位，这意味着在他之后，**男女在所有灵性品位上不存在任何差异**。对于穆罕默德的追随者而言，男性专属的灵性品位从未实际存在过：

> "After the death of the Prophet Muhammad, who, according to Muslim tradition, has sealed the station of prophecy, there is no difference in men's and women's access to all spiritual stations." (p. 210)

> "Historically, therefore, Muslim women have always held *exactly* the same capacity and access to spiritual stations as have men." (p. 210)

### 7. 案例五：女性领拜（imamate）

伊本·阿拉比在女性领拜问题上采取了大胆而激进的立场：

> "'Some people allow the imamate of women absolutely before a congregation of men and women. I agree with this. Some forbid her imamate absolutely. Others permit her imamate in a congregation exclusively of women...The basic principle is allowing women's imamate. Thus whoever asserts that it is forbidden without proof, he should be ignored. The one who forbids this has no explicit text.'" (Ibn Arabi, 1911, vol. 1, p. 447; 引于 p. 211)

**法学推导方法**——伊本·阿拉比的"灵性法律话语"（spiritual legal discourse）：

1. 将公共社区角色与个人灵性能力联系起来——先知已证明某些女性的灵性完美（kamal），而先知就是领袖，因此女性的领拜地位是健全的。
2. **基本原则（asl）是允许**——禁止者没有明确经文依据，仅凭对女性的"共同的[负面]看法"，这一证据"不成立"（insubstantial）。
3. Eric Winkel (1997) 将此方法描述为"灵性法律话语"——辨识神圣指引，"照亮从外在仪式到内在真理的跨越"。

> "Ibn Arabi explicitly connects the Prophet's affirmation of women's spiritual capacity to ritual leadership and explicitly disregards the position of scholars who reject women's imamate." (p. 211–212)

## 六、教法学推导方法总结

| 方法 | 内容 | 法律意义 |
|---|---|---|
| **宗教人类学批判** | 揭示教法学背后对"人"的性别化假设 | 为从根本上重构法律主体提供基础 |
| **Shari'ah/fiqh 区分** | 沙里亚是道德愿景，教法学是人类解释尝试 | 教法学的性别等级不具有超历史的宗教权威 |
| **灵性能力→法律能力** | 灵性平等直接转化为社会和法律领域的平等 | 为女性法律证词、领拜、设立先例提供依据 |
| **本体论优先于社会惯例** | 以古兰经原型叙事确定本体论边界，社会习俗为偶然性事项 | 衣着遮羞范围男女一致，端庄要求是社会生成的 |
| **经验/知识基础决定法律能力** | 证词效力取决于经验领域而非性别 | 随社会变迁动态调整法律规则 |
| **Jamal（慈悯）优先于 jalal（威严）** | 真主的慈悯先于怒火，人类应优先体现美的属性 | 法律体系应体现关怀与正义伦理 |
| **先知封印论证** | 先知穆罕默德封印先知品位后男女灵性品位无差异 | 男性专属灵性品位在实际上从未适用于穆斯林社区 |
| **基本原则（asl）论证** | 允许（ibaha）是基本原则，禁止需要明确证据 | 无明确经文禁止则女性领拜等权力应被允许 |

## 七、结论

Shaikh 认为，支撑穆斯林法律的"女性"和"男性"的宗教建构，应当在本质上与伊斯兰核心的最深层形而上学观念以及人与真主的交往本质相联系。教法学需要被基础性的神学考量所照亮——即对真主的本质和性别包容的人类存在的理解。

> "The religious constructions of 'women' and 'men' underpinning Muslim laws should, by definition, be linked to the deepest metaphysical conceptions and the nature of human engagement with God which lie at the heart of Islam." (p. 213)

> "In all contexts, Islamic legal formulations need to be illuminated by foundational theological considerations of the nature of God and a gender-inclusive humanity." (p. 213)

苏菲传统中关于人类可能性之完全平等的观点深深扎根于伊斯兰核心神学事业之中，尽管它们在主流中被忽视和边缘化：

> "Such religious anthropologies are part of the Muslim tradition – indeed, they are thoroughly immersed in the core theological enterprise of Islam – even if they have been neglected and marginalized in the mainstream." (p. 213)
