# Asma Lamrabet: An Egalitarian Reading of the Concepts of Khilafah, Wilayah and Qiwamah

## 基本信息

- **作者**: Asma Lamrabet
- **原文语言**: 法语（由 Amina Chibani 和 Richard Tapper 译为英语）
- **古兰经译文**: 默认采用 Abdullah Yusuf Ali 译本，另有作者自译
- **页码范围**: 118–152

---

## 一、总论点

Lamrabet 主张：伊斯兰灵性信息中关于男女关系的核心是**正义与平等不可分割**（justice and equality are inseparable）。她通过重新解读三个古兰经概念——**伊斯提赫拉夫**（istikhlaf，代治）、**维拉亚**（wilayah，共同责任）和**基瓦玛**（qiwamah，管理/供养）——来论证这一点。

> "The spiritual message of Islam regarding men and women is one in which justice and equality are inseparable." (p. 120)

她批判两个阵营：一是固守僵化解读的伊斯兰传统主义者，二是拒绝任何伊斯兰参照的世俗女权倡导者，认为需要走出两者之间的对立，提出当代改革主义视野。

---

## 二、方法论：古兰经经文的分类框架

Lamrabet 提出一个规范性框架（normative framework），将古兰经经文分为三类：

### 1. 具有普世目标的经文（Verses with universal aims）

倡导正义、平等、公正和人的尊严等普世价值，构成古兰经信息的基础。

> "These verses advocate universal values such as justice, equality, fairness and respect for human dignity. They place the human (*al-insan*) at the centre of all spiritual philosophy. The majority of verses are universal, and these constitute the basis of the Qur'anic message." (p. 121)

### 2. 受限于启示语境的临时性经文（Temporary verses）

与七世纪阿拉伯半岛的特定历史时期相关，如战利品分配、奴隶制或肉刑。

> "These verses are linked to a given historical period and place, namely that of seventh-century Arabia. They met the specific requirements of that context but can be considered obsolete today." (p. 121)

其价值不在于字面含义或适用，而在于其**目的**（aims）和**正义精神**（spirit of justice）。

### 3. 需要在新社会语境中重新解释的经文

涉及在"启示时代"（time of revelation）的父权制期望下处理女性问题的经文，如婚姻和家庭。

> "These verses...sometimes endorse discriminatory attitudes and practices of the time or reform such practices without actually repealing them." (p. 122)

**核心批判**：大多数传统解经混淆了这三类经文，将临时性经文或特定社会性经文置于普世经文之上，损害了古兰经的灵性信息。

> "Most interpretations confound these three types of verses and prioritize those which are temporary or those concerned with specific social dimensions over the universal ones." (p. 122)

## 三、伊斯提赫拉夫（Istikhlaf）：建设人类文明中的平等

### 3.1 概念来源

Istikhlaf 源自古兰经中多处出现的词根，至少有三层含义：

1. **继承/哈里发**（khilafah）：继任者占据他人位置（如 6:165, 10:14, 10:73, 2:30, 38:26）
2. **复数形式 khala'if**：民族、部落、世代的相继（如 7:69, 7:74, 7:150, 7:169）
3. **代治责任**：真主委托人类管理世界的责任

核心经文为 2:30：

> "'Behold, thy Lord said to the angels: "I will create a vicegerent on earth." They said: "Wilt Thou place therein one who will make mischief therein and shed blood?..." He said: "I know what ye know not."'" (p. 123)

### 3.2 教法学推导

**绝大多数经注学家**（exegetes）一致认为"大地上的代治者"指的是**人类全体**（bani adam）。

> "The vast majority of exegetes agree that by 'vicegerent on earth' the Qur'an meant human beings (*bani adam*) or the human race in God's first initiative to populate the earth (Ibn Kathir, 2003, vol. 1, p. 80)." (p. 123)

人类——男人和女人——被赋予理性，因此被造物主选择承担在大地上繁衍和安排生存条件的最高职能。

> "Human beings, men and women, were endowed with reason and therefore chosen by the Creator to undertake this supreme function of populating the earth and arranging the conditions of their existence in this world." (p. 124)

### 3.3 历史政治扭曲

哈里发概念经历了从灵性到政治的篡夺：

- 从**真主委托全体人类的灵性责任**
- 到**当选政治代表**
- 再到**真主在大地上的法定代表**

> "The concept of caliph has passed from the spiritual sense of a responsibility delegated by God to all humans, to that of an elected political representative, to the supposed legal representative of God on earth." (p. 124)

学者们授权使用"真主的哈里发"头衔，使统治者的神圣性合法化，不服从统治者等同于不服从真主。加之父权社会规范和薄弱圣训的推动，**只有男性能够成为统治者和履行这一神圣责任**成为了"自然的"认知。

> "Because of existing patriarchal social norms coupled with the promotion of weak *ahadith* and supposedly 'inherent' characteristics of men and women...it became natural that only men could be rulers and fulfil this divine responsibility." (pp. 124–125)

### 3.4 法律实践意义

Lamrabet 认为，恢复 khilafah 的原初灵性含义意味着：

> "Equality for men and women should be read to mean equality in the responsibility and corresponding liberty to choose, to work, to manage and to participate in the progress of society and the world in which they live." (p. 125)

## 四、维拉亚（Wilayah）：男女共同承担的责任

### 4.1 核心经文

古兰经 9:71：

> "The believers, men and women, are *awliya'* [allies] of one another. They enjoin *al-ma'ruf* [the good] and forbid *al-munkar* [the bad], they observe *salat* [prayers] and give *zakat* [charitable alms] and obey God and His Prophet." (p. 126)

### 4.2 语义分析

*Awliya'*（单数 *wali*）的语义场包括：

- **结盟、互助、相互强化**（alliance, mutual assistance, mutual reinforcement）
- *Al-wali* 是真主的尊名之一，意为强化者、保护者、支持者
- 日常用法中，*wali* 也指**管理者、监护人**，可以管理、治理、受托事务
- *Al-wala'*：亲近、友谊、忠诚
- *Al-wilayah*：法律术语中指**最高政治治理职位**（wilayah 'ammah，一般治理），包括哈里发、法官（al-qadi）、财务官（al-muhtasib）

> "There is also *al-wilayah*, mostly known in legal parlance as denoting the highest positions in political governance, which Muslim jurists referred to as *wilayah 'ammah* or general governance." (pp. 126–127)

### 4.3 传统经注的局限

Lamrabet 指出，这一明确呼吁男女平等的经文在伊斯兰思想中被**边缘化**：

> "Some very explicit universal verses such as this, which exhort equality between men and women, have been marginalized in Islamic thought. They are rarely cited and are even at times completely overlooked." (p. 126)

传统经注虽然承认男女在**崇拜中的灵性平等**，但这是唯一被承认的平等。大多数注释家试图在崇拜平等与传统性别等级和互补之间寻找宗教折衷。

> "Most commentators try to find a religious compromise between gender equality in worship practices and traditional gender hierarchy and complementarity as the norm in most other cases." (pp. 127–128)

### 4.4 伊本·凯西尔的两个重要观察

**第一**：9:71 中描述的男女联盟（wilayah）与先知的一则著名圣训完全一致：

> "'All believers are united, generous and forgiving to each other, just like the human body; as soon as one of its organs suffers from any disease, the whole body reacts with fever and pain' (Ibn Kathir, 2003, vol. 2, p. 350)." (p. 128)

**第二**：该经文中"enjoin the common good"的命令与 3:104 中的相同：

> "'Let there arise out of you a band of people inviting to all that is good, enjoining what is right [*al-ma'ruf*] and forbidding what is wrong [*al-munkar*]; They are the ones to attain felicity.'" (p. 129)

### 4.5 经文间解释的不一致（教法学关键问题）

Lamrabet 揭示了一个核心矛盾：

- 当"enjoin the common good and forbid the bad"出现在 3:104、3:110、3:114 等经文中时，注释家们将其解释为**社会政治行动的义务**
- 但当**完全相同的命令**出现在 9:71（明确提及男女）时，注释家们**仅仅将其解释为崇拜中的灵性平等**

> "But it is surprising to find that this same command, when found in verse 9:71 about *wilayah* and the alliance between men and women, does not lead to the same commentaries." (p. 130)

> "When the exact same injunction is addressed to people or believers in general in other verses (such as 3:104, 3:110 or 3:114), exegetes have interpreted it as a prescription towards social justice concerning men only." (p. 130)

### 4.6 改革主义学者的佐证

- **拉希德·里达**（Rashid Rida, 1865–1935）："This verse makes a point of inviting women as well as men to enjoin good and forbid evil actions. This includes speech and writing but also criticism of political leaders, caliphs, kings, princes and other persons." (Aboû Chouqqa, 2007, vol. 2, p. 646; 引于 p. 131)

- **艾哈迈德·库夫塔罗**（Ahmed Kuftaro, 1912–2004）："Allah deliberately mentioned women in this verse in order to insist on their effective participation, knowing that the term '*mu'minin*', believers, conventionally includes women." (Kuftaro, 2002; 引于 p. 131)

- **法德拉拉**（Fadlallah, 1935–2010）："Men and women are partners in building up life. And just like men are responsible of the society, women also are equally responsible of the society they live in." (Fadlallah, n.d.a; 引于 p. 131)

### 4.7 法律实践意义

Lamrabet 将 wilayah 概念整合入**平等公民身份框架**：

> "The call of the Qur'an to this shared responsibility of women and men in the political management of society is the equivalent of a key democratic principle requiring equal responsibility of all citizens. Thus, the concept of *wilayah* could be understood as a corollary of equal citizenship in Muslim societies." (p. 132)

她指出，wilayah 的社会政治参与含义在历史上因专制政权而衰退，被降格为**宗教监护**（al-wilayah al-diniyah）。

> "From its universal political and intellectual dimensions, it was downgraded to a strictly delimited area of religious ritual, *al-wilayah al-diniyah* (religious guardianship) (Laroui, 2008, p. 158)." (p. 132)

## 五、基瓦玛（Qiwamah）：男女对公私空间的管理

### 5.1 概念来源与词根分析

*Qiwamah* 源自古兰经术语 *qawwamun*/*qawwamin*（名词 *qawwam* 的复数形式），来自词根 *qama*，在阿拉伯语中有多达**三十种含义**：站立、遵守、承担、进行、起来、供养、反抗、忍耐、举起等。

> "This comes from the root *qama*, which in Arabic may have as many as thirty meanings: 'stand up', 'comply', 'carry', 'take on', 'proceed', 'rise up', 'provide for', 'revolt', 'endure', 'lift up', etc." (p. 134)

### 5.2 三处出现 qawwamun/qawwamin 的经文

**经文一（4:34）——最常被引用**：

> "Men are *qawwamun* over women according to what God *faddala ba'dahum 'ala ba'd* [has favoured some over others] and according to what they spend from their wealth. Righteous women are *qanitat* [obedient] guarding the unseen according to what God has guarded. Those [women] whose *nushuz* [rebellion] you fear, admonish them, and abandon them in bed, and *adribuhunna* [strike them]." (p. 134)

**经文二（4:135）——公共领域**：

> "O ye who believe! *Kunu qawwamin* [stand out firmly] *bilqisti* [for justice], as witnesses to Allah, even as against yourselves, or your parents, or your kin..." (pp. 134–135)

**经文三（5:8）——公共领域**：

> "O ye who believe! *Kunu qawwamin* [stand out firmly] *lillahi shuhada' bilqisti* [for Allah, as witnesses to fair dealing], and let not the hatred of others to you make you swerve to wrong and depart from justice." (p. 135)

### 5.3 公私二分法

Lamrabet 援引 Heba Raof Ezzat (2000) 的区分：

- **公共基瓦玛**（qiwamah 'ammah）：4:135 和 5:8 中，面向全体信士
- **私人基瓦玛**（qiwamah khassah）：4:34 中，涉及夫妻关系

### 5.4 公共基瓦玛的教法学分析

两节经文中的命令：
- 4:135: "*kunu qawwamin bilqisti*"（在实施正义方面坚定严格）
- 5:8: "*kunu qawwamin lilahi shuhada'*"（在作证方面坚定精确）

Lamrabet 认为这才是 *qawwamun* 的**真正含义**：

> "This is the true meaning of the term *qawwamun*, which stipulates an impartial moral consciousness and a perfect rigour in fairness and testimony." (p. 136)

先知的一段高品级圣训（marfu' hadith）佐证了正义的首要地位：

> "'An hour of justice equates to sixty years of devotional practices [*'ibadat*]' (Abi Ennaim, n.d., p. 117)." (p. 137)

### 5.5 私人基瓦玛的传统经注（批判）

几乎所有经典和当代经注都将 *qawwamun* 译为"权威"或"管理"。

> "Most translations of verse 4:34 render the word *qawwamun* as 'authority' or 'management': 'Men have authority over women' or 'Men are in charge of managing women's affairs'." (p. 137)

经典注释的具体用语：

- 伊本·凯西尔（Ibn Kathir, 2003, vol. 1, p. 605）："*al-rajul qaymun 'ala el-mar'aa, ay huwa ra'isaha wa kabiraha wa al-hakim 'alayha wa mu'adibuha*"（男人是女人的管事者，即她的主人、她的上级、她的统治者、她的管教者）(p. 151, 注释 20)

- 伊本·阿巴斯（Ibn Abbas）：男性对女性道德生活的控制或霸权（*musalatun 'ala adab al-nisa*）(p. 137)

- 扎马赫沙里（Al-Zamakhshari, n.d., vol. 1, pp. 441–2）：男性有权在所有社会生活领域支配女性（*musaytirin 'alayhin*）(p. 137)

- 伊本·盖伊姆（Ibn al-Qayyim, 1991, vol. 2, p. 67）：**将妻子比作俘虏或奴隶**，应服从拥有绝对权力的丈夫 (p. 137)

传统注释中为男性优越性辩护的"证据"包括(pp. 138–139)：
- 男性天生理性更强，女性因过度情感化而理性不足
- 只有男性担任重大政治和司法职位
- 先知身份仅属于男性
- 男性继承份额是女性的两倍
- 多妻制是男性权利和优越性的证明
- 单方面离婚是男性专属权利

### 5.6 历史政治语境中的扭曲

Lamrabet 揭示了 qiwamah 解释的**政治根源**：

> "This interpretation of the concept of *qiwamah* was imagined and developed in the context of political developments whose objectives were completely removed from the emancipatory spirit conveyed by the message of the Qur'an." (p. 140)

丈夫的权威被明确**比拟为国家元首**（hakim）的权威：

> "Qiwamah was construed according to the reading of *hakimiya* (political governance), since the husband's authority was explicitly likened to that of the *hakim* (head of state)." (p. 140)

由于当时的政治治理是专制的，qiwamah 因此成为**家庭专制**（tasalut）的同义词：

> "As political governance at the time was autocratic and despotic, *qiwamah*, by extrapolation, became synonymous with familial despotism (*tasalut*)." (p. 140)

这一丈夫服从-政治服从的链条被视为**同一枚硬币的两面**，并被赋予了神圣秩序的色彩。

### 5.7 重新解读：qawwamun 的真正含义

Lamrabet 认为 *qawwamun* 实际意为**供养者/支持者**（provider or supporter）：

> "However, the word *qawwamun*, which was erroneously translated as 'authority', actually means provider or supporter in the context of the verse." (p. 141)

经文后半句"因为他们从自己的财产中花费"表明，男性之所以是 qawwamun，是因为**他们用资源供养配偶和家庭**。

### 5.8 "bima faddala ba'dahum 'ala ba'd"的语法分析

这一短语通常被翻译为"因为安拉使他们（男性）优越于她们（女性）"，但 Lamrabet 指出其**字面翻译**并无性别指定：

> "However, a literal translation would be, 'because of the favours Allah has given to one over the other' (without a gender specification) – favours granted to some men or women over some other men or women." (p. 141)

如果经文意在指定性别，应使用 "*bima faddala lahu ba'dahum 'ala ba'dihina*"。

穆罕默德·阿卜杜（Muhammad 'Abduh）的解释被援引：

> "This verse stipulates that some men are favoured over some women, and some women are favoured over some men. This means a mutual preference in accordance with the Qur'an's spirit of fairness. (Reda, 1999, vol. 5, p. 55)." (pp. 141–142)

### 5.9 qiwamah 是责任而非荣耀

> "*Qiwamah* is not an honour (*tashrif*) but a responsibility (*taklif*), unlike what patriarchal interpretations claim when they deduce the innate superiority of men." (p. 142)

赛义德·库特布（Sayed Qutb, 2008）将此概念**严格限定在婚姻制度内**：

> "'In order to organize the institution of marriage and clarify the functions of its members, the qiwamah is conferred in this text upon man' (Qutb, 2008, p. 649)." (p. 142)

阿卜杜和沙勒图特（Shaltut）认为 qiwamah 赋予丈夫**不是因为性别，而仅因其家庭财务管理者的身份**。如果妻子承担了这一供养职能——在现代社会中经常如此——她就应行使 qiwamah。

> "This means that if this maintenance function were to fall on the wife – as is often the case in modern societies – she should exercise this *qiwamah*; thus no male exclusivity is involved." (pp. 142–143)

### 5.10 先知的实践

布哈里圣训集记载，阿伊莎被问及先知在家中做什么，她回答：

> "He would serve his family and when it was time for prayer he would get up and pray." (al-Bukhari, 1987, vol. 1, p. 159; 引于 p. 145)

在伊玛目艾哈迈德和伊本·希班的类似圣训中，阿伊莎说先知会做缝衣、补器皿、挤奶等日常家务。

> "The Prophet's *qiwamah* was to be at his family's service; it was not authoritarianism, let alone despotism." (p. 145)

**重要的教法学观察**：古兰经中**没有任何经文**将特定的家务分配给某一性别：

> "No Qur'anic verse allocates specific household tasks or functions to either of the sexes...Not a single verse mentions domestic work as solely a task for women." (p. 145)

### 5.11 qiwamah 与 ta'a（服从）的问题

qiwamah 及其推论——服从（ta'a）——催生了大量贬低女性的宗教文献：

> "*Qiwamah* and its corollary obedience (*ta'a*) have facilitated the proliferation of a whole religious literature that devalues women." (p. 139)

妻子对丈夫的服从被说成是通往天堂的途径，这与伊斯兰的**基本原则**相矛盾：**服从和顺从只归于真主**。

> "This contradicts one of the founding principles of Islam, namely that obedience and submission are due only to God. It questions the unity of God (*tawhid*), a major notion on which the whole spiritual dimension of Islam rests (Wadud, 2009)." (p. 140)

### 5.12 ma'ruf（公益/善行）的对比

*Ma'ruf* 在古兰经中出现**多次**（2:178, 2:180, 2:228, 2:232, 2:233, 2:234, 2:236, 2:241, 3:104, 3:110, 3:114, 4:6, 4:19, 7:157, 9:67, 9:71, 9:112, 22:41, 31:17），而 *qiwamah* 一词**从未出现**，衍生词 *qawwamun* 在婚姻关系中**仅出现一次**。

> "The term *ma'ruf* occurs numerous times in the Qur'an...while the term *qiwamah* is not mentioned at all, and the term *qawammun* from which *qiwamah* is derived is mentioned only once in regard to marital relations." (pp. 143–144)

然而 qiwamah 在阿拉伯-穆斯林思想中获得了与 ma'ruf 完全不成比例的重要性。

## 六、结论：为正义而站立

三个概念应当**联合阅读**（read together as standing for justice）：

| 概念 | 含义 | 范围 |
|------|------|------|
| **Istikhlaf/Khilafah** | 每个人受托管理大地上的生活 | 人类全体 |
| **Wilayah** | 男女共同承担行善止恶的公共责任 | 公共/社会政治领域 |
| **Qiwamah（公共）** | 对正义和公正的道德责任 | 全体信士 |
| **Qiwamah（私人）** | 丈夫在婚姻中的财务供养责任 | 核心家庭 |

> "To reduce *wilayah* to male guardianship over dependent wards or *qiwamah* to an assumed authority of the husband amounts to violating the spiritual principles of the Qur'anic message regarding the ethics of marriage and family life." (p. 147)

### 法律实践实例：摩洛哥 2004 年家庭法改革

> "In 2004 the new Moroccan Law of the Family (*Mudawwana*) replaced the old notion of 'the husband as head of the family' with co-responsibility and placed the family under the joint supervision of both spouses." (p. 152, 注释 26)
