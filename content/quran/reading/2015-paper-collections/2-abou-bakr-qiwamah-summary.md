# 《作为经注建构的"齐瓦玛"之诠释遗产》详细总结

**原文标题**: *The Interpretive Legacy of Qiwamah as an Exegetical Construct*
**作者**: Omaima Abou-Bakr
**页码**: 84–117

---

## 一、研究目标

本文旨在追溯古兰经术语 *qawwāmūn*（قوّامون）在经注（*tafsīr*）文献中的诠释积累过程，揭示其含义在历史长河中发生的演变、转移与固化。核心问题：

> "How exactly did the Qur'anic sentence '*al-rijāl qawwāmūn ʿalā al-nisāʾ bimā faḍḍala Allāh baʿḍahum ʿalā baʿḍ wa bimā anfaqū min amwālihim*' … become an independent and separate (transcontextual) patriarchal construct?"
>
> "古兰经这句'男人是女人的保护者/供养者，因为安拉使他们中的一些优于另一些，又因他们以其财产供养她们'……是如何变成一个独立的、脱离语境的父权建构的？"（p. 84）

---

## 二、从描述到规范：四个话语阶段

Abou-Bakr 将 *qawwāmūn* 向父权化 *qiwāmah* 建构的演变归纳为**四个阶段**（p. 86）：

### 第一阶段：从描述性到规范性（塔巴里，卒于923年）

**核心转变**: 将描述性的 *qawwāmūn* 转化为规范性/规定性概念，标志是语法上从动词形式转为动名词（*maṣdar*）*qiyām*，后发展为 *qiwāmah*。

塔巴里（al-Ṭabarī）使用了短语：

> "*ahl qiyām ʿalā nisāʾihim* (watching over or being in charge of their women) to establish men's collective task or responsibility of *qiyām* in disciplining women"
>
> "他使用了'管理/照看其妇女的人'这一短语，确立了男性对女性进行纪律管束（*taʾdīb*）的集体任务或责任"（p. 88）

**教法学意义**: 塔巴里将经济供养的神圣指令扭曲为特权的理由：

> "not only did al-Tabari initiate and put into motion the hierarchal idea of moral superiority and the right to discipline (*taʾdībihinna*), but he also instituted the twisted logic of turning the divine assignment to provide economic support into a reason for privilege: 'they provide because they are better, or they are better because they provide'."
>
> "塔巴里不仅发起并推动了道德优越性和管教权（*taʾdībihinna*）的等级观念，还建立了一种扭曲的逻辑——将供养的神圣指令变成特权的理由：'他们供养是因为他们更优越，或者他们更优越是因为他们供养。'"（p. 88–89）

**原始含义的四重偏移**（p. 89）:
1. 从描述性变为规范性 / 从责任变为权威
2. 引入名词 *qiyām*（为后来的 *qiwāmah* 铺路），作为本质主义的道德优越观念
3. 从提供经济供养的特定含义，扩展为所有男性在任何时间和地点的普遍地位
4. 从因遗产而产生的相对的、可变的物质优裕条件，变为基于性别的无条件偏爱

引用 Amina Wadud：

> "what Allah has preferred is restricted to the material, and not absolute"
>
> "安拉所偏爱的仅限于物质方面，并非绝对的"（Wadud 1999, p. 71；引于 p. 89）

### 第二阶段：通过堆积理由进行固化（扎马赫沙里，卒于1144年）

扎马赫沙里（al-Zamakhsharī）在其 *Al-Kashshāf* 中做了两件事：

1. **引入政治隐喻**：将男女关系比作"统治者"与"臣民"的关系：

> "he consolidated superiority and the right to discipline by introducing a metaphor that likened the relationship between men and women to the relationship between 'rulers' and 'subjects', in that they 'manage them by ordering and forbidding' (*āmirīn, nāhīn*)"
>
> "他通过引入一个将男女关系比作'统治者'与'臣民'关系的隐喻来巩固优越性和管教权——即他们'通过命令和禁止来管理她们'（*āmirīn, nāhīn*）"（p. 90）

2. **提供一长串教法学理由**（p. 90）:

> "men are better in 'reason, resoluteness, determination, strength, writing (in most cases), horsemanship, spear-throwing'; among them are 'the prophets and the ulama'; further, 'they perform the *imāmah*, the *jihād*, the *adhān*, the *khuṭbah*, *iʿtikāf*, loud *takbīrāt* … bearing witness in *ḥudūd* and *qiṣāṣ*, increase of inheritance shares and divisions, *wilāyah* in marriage, the right to pronounce divorce and revoke it, the number of spouses, lineage, in addition to having beards and wearing turbans'."
>
> "男性在'理性、坚定、决心、力量、书写（多数情况下）、马术、投枪'方面更优越；他们中有'先知和学者'；此外，'他们执行领拜（*imāmah*）、圣战（*jihād*）、唤拜（*adhān*）、布道（*khuṭbah*）、静修（*iʿtikāf*）、高声念大赞词……在刑法（*ḥudūd*）和报复法（*qiṣāṣ*）中作证、更多的遗产份额和分配、婚姻中的监护权（*wilāyah*）、宣布离婚和撤回离婚的权利、配偶数量、血统，以及蓄须和戴缠头巾'。"（p. 90）

作者评论：

> "Notice the mixed bag of innate qualities, social customs and *fiqhī* deductions, a confusing and arbitrary mix that has stayed with us for centuries."
>
> "注意这是一个混杂了先天素质、社会习俗和教法学推导的大杂烩，一种令人困惑和武断的混合物，数百年来一直伴随着我们。"（p. 90）

### 第三阶段：扩展与积累——从家庭到公共领域

#### 拉齐（al-Rāzī，卒于1209年）

拉齐最初将该经文回归到经济/遗产层面，提出了一个相当合理的解释：

> "'Although both men and women share their mutual enjoyment of one another, God ordered men to pay to them dower and sustenance; hence, the increase in one side is met by an increase in the other side, so as if no excess (*faḍl*) whatsoever'"
>
> "'尽管男女共享彼此的欢愉，安拉命令男性向她们支付聘礼和生活费；因此，一方的增加被另一方的增加所抵消，因而实际上没有任何多余（*faḍl*）'"（al-Rāzī 1938, vol. 10, p. 87；引于 p. 91）

**教法学推导**: 拉齐讨论了安拉为何赋予"男性对女性的权威/主权"（*salṭanah*），列出两大原因：

> "The first reason that men are better than women was a combination of intrinsic (*ḥaqīqiyyah*) characteristics and in *sharʿī* rules: knowledge, mental capacity, physical ability, resoluteness, horsemanship, spear-throwing, being prophets, *ulamāʾ* and imams, undertaking *jihād*, the *adhān*, the *khuṭbah*, bearing witness, more inheritance shares, being charged with blood-money, with *wilāyah* in marriage, divorce, and number of wives, and lineage."
>
> "第一个原因——男性优于女性——是内在（*ḥaqīqiyyah*）特征和教法规则的结合：知识、心智能力、体力、坚定、马术、投枪、身为先知/学者/伊玛目、承担圣战、唤拜、布道、作证、更多遗产份额、承担血金义务、婚姻监护权（*wilāyah*）、离婚权、配偶数量和血统。"（p. 91–92）

#### 白达维（al-Bayḍāwī，卒于1286年）

白达维将标准化表达固化——男性"如同统治臣民的君主"：

> "the notion of men 'being their [women's] sovereign like rulers over subjects'"（1968, vol. 1, p. 217；引于 p. 92）

**法律实践创新**: 区分了**天赋的**（*wahbī*）和**获得的**（*kasbī*）优越原因，并引入离婚方面的新表述：

> "*al-istibdād bi-l-furaq* (i.e. independently and autocratically taking the decision of parting)"
>
> "*al-istibdād bi-l-furaq*（即独立而专断地做出分离的决定）"（p. 92）

#### 古尔图比（al-Qurṭubī，卒于1273年）

古尔图比从马利基法学视角出发，将 *qiwāmah* 与遗产份额（4:11节）联系起来：

> "God showed the reason for this increased share in inheritance: it was because husbands paid dower and provided for women; hence, 'the benefit of this preference goes back to them [women]'"
>
> "安拉展示了遗产份额增加的原因：因为丈夫支付了聘礼并供养女性；因此，'这种优待的利益回归到她们（女性）身上'"（al-Qurṭubī 1967, vols 5–6, p. 168；引于 p. 93）

**引入伪科学证据**:

> "men's disposition is determined by heat and dryness which gives them strength and hardness, whereas women's disposition is determined by humidness and coldness, giving them the characteristics of leniency and weakness"
>
> "男性的体质由热和干决定，赋予他们力量和坚硬；而女性的体质由湿和冷决定，赋予她们温柔和软弱的特征"（al-Qurṭubī 1967, vols 5–6, p. 169；引于 p. 93）

**关键法律争议**: 古尔图比提到一种法学理解——若丈夫无力供养，则他不是妻子的 *qawwām*：

> "if a husband was incapable of providing, then he would not be *qawwām* over his wife – meaning that he can be sovereign only when he provides"
>
> "如果丈夫无力供养，他就不是妻子的 *qawwām*——意味着他只有在供养时才能拥有主权"（p. 94）

作者指出这暴露了 *qiwāmah* 概念内部的两难：

> "Notice the confusion, back and forth, between two notions of *qiwāmah*. Contingent upon financial support or an absolute condition? An injunction to spend or a feature of divine preference?"
>
> "注意两种 *qiwāmah* 概念之间的来回混淆：是以经济供养为条件的，还是绝对的状态？是花费的命令，还是神圣偏爱的特征？"（p. 94）

#### 伊本·凯西尔（Ibn Kathīr，1301–1373年）

**从家庭领域扩展到公共领域**:

> "Ibn Kathir expanded the notion of men's superiority through the use of Abu-Bakara's famous *hadith* about women's leadership (No people will prosper that has delegated a woman to lead their affairs), and he stipulated that women cannot be judges in the context of verse 4:34, thereby extending the scope of male supremacy and authority from the domestic to the public sphere."
>
> "伊本·凯西尔利用阿布·巴卡拉的著名圣训——关于女性领导权的（'让女人治理其事务的民族不会成功'）——扩展了男性优越观念，并在4:34节的语境下规定女性不能担任法官，从而将男性至上权威的范围从家庭领域扩展到了公共领域。"（p. 94–95）

**法律方法论**: 伊本·凯西尔将4:34节与2:228节的 *darajah*（等级）概念联系起来，并强迫性地将所有关于妻子顺从丈夫的圣训编纂在一起（p. 95）。

#### 苏尤提（al-Suyūṭī，卒于1505年）

**圣训堆积的顶峰**: 仅4:34节就用了六页，引用了27条圣训或类比引文：

> "The majority of *aḥādīth* cited were weak (mainly from the less authoritative collections) and the excessive accumulation and repetition of negative pronouncements on women, ascribed to the Prophet or the Companions, recall typical medieval misogynistic literature found in European traditions."
>
> "所引圣训大多为弱传述（主要来自权威性较低的汇编），对女性的消极言论过度累积和重复，让人想起欧洲传统中典型的中世纪厌女文学。"（p. 97）

**首次出现在经注作品中的极端圣训**（p. 97）:
- 妻子向丈夫"叩首"的隐喻
- "舔溃疡和脓液"
- "即使在骆驼背上也要回应丈夫的要求"

**社会历史背景**: 作者将苏尤提的极端语气与马穆鲁克时期女性在公共领域的广泛出现相联系：

> "Are these religious texts simply a conservative reaction to contemporaneous social reality and a growing desire to limit women's movements in the public arena?"
>
> "这些宗教文本是否只是对当时社会现实的保守反应，以及限制女性在公共领域活动的日益增长的愿望？"（p. 97）

### 第四阶段：现代主义——家庭意识形态与生物本质主义

#### 穆罕默德·阿卜杜（Muḥammad ʿAbduh，卒于1905年）

**引入"天性"（*fiṭrah*）论证**:

> "He introduced the notion of 'the *fitrī* reason' of *qiwāmah*"
>
> "他引入了 *qiwāmah* 的'天性理由'概念"（ʿAbduh 1990, pp. 55–58；引于 p. 98）

**构建"领导-被领导"关系**:

> "'Abduh particularly elaborated on the compound notion of *riyāsah–raʾīs–marʾūs* (i.e. rule/leadership–leader–ruled), constructing the marital relationship as a boss–subordinate relationship."
>
> "阿卜杜特别阐述了*riyāsah–raʾīs–marʾūs*（即统治/领导——领导者——被领导者）的复合概念，将婚姻关系建构为上级-下级关系。"（p. 98）

**与古典法学家的矛盾**:

> "'Abduh's pronouncements directly opposed those of the classical jurists, who did not include domestic service as incumbent upon women in marriage."
>
> "阿卜杜的主张与古典法学家直接对立——后者并不将家务劳动列为婚姻中女性的义务。"（p. 99）

#### 赛义德·库特布（Sayyid Quṭb，卒于1966年）

> "He reasoned that, like in any other formal organization, it is necessary that parties in the family perform their 'specialized functions' according to their 'capabilities and readiness'."
>
> "他推理说，如同任何其他正式组织一样，家庭中的各方有必要根据其'能力和准备情况'执行其'专门功能'。"（Quṭb 1952, pp. 57–60；引于 p. 100）

## 三、改革主义诠释策略

### 1. 有条件的 *qiwāmah*（以阿伊莎·泰穆尔为先驱）

泰穆尔（A'isha Taymur，1840–1902）通过寓言故事提出：

> "Taymur's contribution was to shed light on a conditioned, shifting *qiwāmah*, contingent on men fulfilling their material and moral responsibilities. She viewed financial support of women as a *condition of*, not a reason for, guardianship."
>
> "泰穆尔的贡献在于揭示了一种有条件的、可变动的 *qiwāmah*，取决于男性是否履行其物质和道德责任。她将对女性的经济供养视为监护的*条件*，而非监护的*理由*。"（p. 103）

此观点成为多位当代学者的基石（p. 103）:
- al-Ḥibrī (1982)、Raḍwān (1988; 2005)
- Wadud 的"灵活的"和"获得的" *qiwāmah*（1999）
- Abou El Fadl 将供养者地位与"客观能力"挂钩（2001, p. 210）
- Bayoumi 的"外在的、可能变化的" *qiwāmah*（2009, p. 62）
- Abu Zayd 将 *qiwāmah* 理解为对"我们被敦促去改变的社会现实"的描述（2007, p. 213）

### 2. 文本语境化（al-Fārūqī 与 Barlas）

al-Fārūqī (2000) 的方法：

> "textual evidence does not warrant *qiwāmah* as an endowed state of excellence because the term 'is used within an entire section that starts from the beginning of the same *sūrah* (chapter) with the issue of possessions in conjunction with marriage and inheritance'"
>
> "文本证据不支持将 *qiwāmah* 视为被赋予的卓越地位，因为该术语'被使用在始于同一章（*sūrah*）开头、涉及财产与婚姻和遗产问题的整个段落中'"（p. 85；引于 p. 104）

Barlas (2002):

> "'Even though the Qur'an charges the husband with being the breadwinner, it does not designate him head of the household, especially as the term has been understood in Western feudal cultures'"
>
> "'尽管古兰经要求丈夫承担养家的责任，但并没有指定他为一家之主，尤其是该术语在西方封建文化中的理解方式'"（p. 187；引于 p. 104）

### 3. 道德轨迹法（Abou El Fadl）

> "the status of *qiwāmah* is hinged on a particular operative cause (*ʿillah*), which is the ability to earn and spend. If a man is not supporting the family, if a wife is also contributing financially or has equal earning potential, then a man's *qiwāmah* cannot exist."
>
> "*qiwāmah* 的地位取决于一个特定的有效原因（*ʿillah*），即赚取和花费的能力。如果一个男人没有供养家庭，如果妻子也在经济上做出贡献或有同等的赚钱能力，那么男性的 *qiwāmah* 就不能存在。"（p. 106）

### 4. 超越文本（Wadud 与 Kecia Ali）

Wadud (2006):

> "there exists the 'possibility of refuting the text, to talk back, to even say "no"'"
>
> "存在'反驳文本、回嘴、甚至说"不"的可能性'"（Wadud 2006, p. 191；引于 p. 107）

## 四、结论：*Qiwāmah* 不是什么

> "In the end, this analysis leads us to conclude that *qiwāmah* is not hierarchy, not moral superiority or paternalism, not divine favouritism, not authority or sovereignty, not an absolute, unqualified right to rule and lead, not a fixed honorary status, not the privilege to spend, and not an exclusive right to earn money and be the sole breadwinner."
>
> "最终，这一分析使我们得出结论：*qiwāmah* 不是等级制度，不是道德优越性或家长制，不是神圣的偏爱，不是权威或主权，不是绝对的、无条件的统治和领导权，不是固定的荣誉地位，不是花费的特权，也不是赚钱和成为唯一经济支柱的排他性权利。"（p. 110）

**构建父权诠释的四大策略**（p. 109）:
- a) 概念化——将其变为抽象概念
- b) 将其与前序经文（4:11, 4:12, 4:32, 4:33）的语境分离，使其成为孤立的、自足的原则
- c) 从有限的、特定的经济指令泛化为管辖婚姻关系所有方面的普遍规则和标准
- d) 创造无根据的含义——将花费的责任变为特权、等级和权威的理由
