# 《认主独一的伦理优先于"齐瓦玛"的伦理》详细总结

**原文标题**: *The Ethics of Tawhid over the Ethics of Qiwamah*
**作者**: Amina Wadud
**页码**: 412–439

---

## 一、核心论题

本文是 Amina Wadud 四十余年古兰经性别平等研究的理论总结。作者从个人经历与学术轨迹出发，论证「齐瓦玛」（qiwamah）作为男性权威的教法学建构既非永恒、也非普世，必须以伊斯兰神学的最高原则——「认主独一」（tawhid）为基础进行范式转换，建立平等互惠的性别关系。

> "The goal, however, is not mere deconstruction, but rather building a more just society that fulfils the higher objectives of Islam and Islamic ethics."
>
> "然而，目标并非单纯的解构，而是建设一个更公正的社会，以实现伊斯兰与伊斯兰伦理的更高目标。"（p. 413）

---

## 二、经文基础与教法学推导

### 2.1 古兰经 4:34 ——「齐瓦玛」的唯一经文来源

作者指出，4:34 不仅被用来为家庭暴力辩护，更提供了齐瓦玛作为男性权威、保护和优越性之概念的**唯一经文依据**：

> "This verse is not only used to justify domestic violence – which I have contested through numerous writings (e.g. Wadud, 1998, pp. 69–70; 2006, chapter 6) – but also provides the only textual reference for the idea of *qiwamah* as meaning male authority, protection and superiority."
>
> "这节经文不仅被用来为家庭暴力辩护——我已通过大量著述对此提出异议——而且是将'齐瓦玛'理解为男性权威、保护和优越性之概念的**唯一经文来源**。"（p. 412）

### 2.2 父权语境下的教法学建构

齐瓦玛的法律含义不是经文的固有意义，而是在父权语境中被建构出来的：

> "the meaning given to *qiwamah* and its subsequent legal applications are constructs built within patriarchal contexts. But patriarchy itself – no matter how benevolently conceived – is neither eternal nor universal."
>
> "赋予'齐瓦玛'的含义及其后续法律适用，都是在父权语境中被建构出来的。而父权制本身——无论被设想得多么仁慈——既非永恒、也非普世。"（p. 412–413）

### 2.3 被边缘化的平等经文

作者对照了古兰经中表达**互惠与平等**的夫妻关系经文，指出齐瓦玛的等级叙事在教法学中被特别优先，而平等叙事被边缘化：

> "The idea of male authority over women, drawn from the phrase '*al-rijal qawwamun 'ala al-nisa'*' (men are *qawwamun* over women) in verse 4:34, has been privileged and prioritized over the concept of reciprocity and equality in marital duties and rights as articulated in the Qur'anic phrases '*wa ja'ala baynukum muwaddatan wa rahmah*' (and He has made intimate love and compassion between you) in verse 30:21, and '*hunna libasun lakum wa antum libasun lahunna*' (they are [like protecting] garments one to the other) in verse 2:187."
>
> "从4:34经文'男人是女人的卡瓦蒙（qawwamun）'中推导出的男性对女性的权威观念，被特别优先于古兰经所表述的夫妻义务与权利中的互惠与平等概念——30:21的'他在你们之间缔造了柔情与怜悯'，以及2:187的'她们是你们的衣衫，你们是她们的衣衫'。"（p. 412）

### 2.4 描述性 vs. 规范性经文

作者提出经文区分的方法论原则——古兰经包含描述性和规范性两类经文，4:34 是对特定历史时期家庭组织方式的描述，而非普遍规范：

> "It is my contention that verse 4:34 may describe a scenario popular at the time of revelation, but that it does not prescribe it as an irrefutable and irreplaceable commandment that must be encoded in the law and practised by all who adhere to Islam as their way of life."
>
> "我的论点是，4:34也许描述了降示时代流行的一种情境，但并非将其规定为必须编入法律、由所有信奉伊斯兰之人遵行的不可辩驳和不可替代的诫命。"（p. 430）

> "It was of great consequence that classical *fuqaha* gave so much emphasis to this term when the Qur'an expresses also the rudiments of equitable, reciprocal relations between women and men in the family. These egalitarian postulates were ignored or seen as tangential."
>
> "具有重大后果的是，古典法学家如此强调这一术语，而古兰经同时也表达了家庭中男女之间公平互惠关系的基本原则。这些平等主义公设被忽视或被视为旁枝末节。"（p. 430）

## 三、齐瓦玛的法律内涵与实践

### 3.1 齐瓦玛的法律定义

齐瓦玛在教法学中被建构为男性排他性地为女性提供保护和供养，换取女性的服从：

> "The concept of *qiwamah* entails men providing protection and maintenance for women in exchange for those women's obedience."
>
> "齐瓦玛的概念要求男性为女性提供保护和供养，以换取这些女性的服从。"（p. 415）

### 3.2 齐瓦玛要求不平等关系

> "*Qiwamah* not only requires unequal relations; it also requires men exclusively to be the providers and protectors for women (and children). This implies that women are incapable of providing for or protecting themselves, or men, or children."
>
> "齐瓦玛不仅要求不平等的关系，还要求男性排他性地作为女性（和儿童）的供养者和保护者。这暗示女性没有能力供养或保护自身、男性或儿童。"（p. 429）

### 3.3 古典教法学中的性别从属

古典教法学将女性置于一个以性别等级为基础的法律结构中：

> "Although women's subjugation to men is a common aspect of gender relations in classical *fiqh*, it is not something that must continue to have priority over other constructs of gender relations in Muslim family laws, especially given current lived experiences."
>
> "尽管女性对男性的从属是古典教法中性别关系的普遍面向，但它不必继续优先于穆斯林家庭法中其他性别关系建构，特别是鉴于当前的生活现实。"（p. 425）

### 3.4 齐瓦玛与家庭暴力的法律关联

将齐瓦玛理解为男性权威，直接导致了家庭暴力在法律层面获得"伊斯兰"辩护：

> "The tenacious tendency to hold on to the idea that men have authority increased the occurrence of domestic violence. Although domestic violence cuts across race, class and religion, it manifests in the black community in direct relationship to the history of racism. However, in the African-American Muslim context, it was given (false) justification, such that classical interpretations of verse 4:34 became an 'Islamic' right to beat recalcitrant women."
>
> "固执地坚持男性拥有权威的观念增加了家庭暴力的发生。尽管家庭暴力横跨种族、阶级和宗教，但它在黑人社区中与种族主义历史有着直接关系。然而在非裔美国穆斯林语境中，它获得了（虚假的）辩护，使得4:34的古典解释变成了殴打不驯女性的'伊斯兰'权利。"（p. 417）

### 3.5 齐瓦玛作为"家长制"（qa'im）的法律运作

即便男性未能履行供养义务，齐瓦玛仍被用来赋予男性绝对权威：

> "Even if an African-American Muslim male did not provide sufficiently to support a wife (or wives) and children – let alone extended family members – the family would hold on tenaciously to the *idea* that he was the *qa'im* (head) of the family. As *qa'im* he was due unquestioned obedience and authority. It was not difficult to find cases where the man in the family wanted all the authority even while unable or unwilling to fulfil any financial responsibility."
>
> "即便一位非裔美国穆斯林男性未能充足地供养妻子（或多位妻子）和子女——更不用说大家庭成员——家庭仍会执拗地坚持他是家庭的'家长'（*qa'im*，即一家之主）这一**观念**。作为家长，他理应获得无条件的服从和权威。不难发现，家中的男人想要一切权威，却无力或不愿履行任何经济责任的案例。"（p. 417）

### 3.6 教法学中齐瓦玛与伊斯兰的等同

齐瓦玛被等同于伊斯兰本身，以致任何质疑都被视为反伊斯兰：

> "If they sought a different model – or addressed the lived realities – it would have been taken as a rejection of Islam because the *qiwamah* of men over women had become synonymous with Islam."
>
> "如果她们寻求一种不同的模式——或正视生活现实——那将被视为对伊斯兰的拒绝，因为男性对女性的齐瓦玛已变得与伊斯兰同义。"（p. 418）

### 3.7 供养者迷思的法律与实践脱节

法律理论与生活现实之间存在系统性脱节：

> "I cannot believe how often I heard the refrain, 'In Islam, a Muslim woman's money belongs to her; she does not even have to pay towards her family.' Meanwhile, I never knew a single family where the woman did not also contribute to the family upkeep."
>
> "我无法相信我多少次听到这种老调重弹：'在伊斯兰中，穆斯林女性的财产属于她自己；她甚至不必为家庭支付任何费用。'然而，我从未见过一个女性不同时为家庭开支做贡献的家庭。"（p. 419）

## 四、非裔美国穆斯林的历史经验与齐瓦玛

### 4.1 奴隶制对齐瓦玛理想的瓦解

奴隶制从根本上消除了齐瓦玛得以运作的前提条件：

> "As an institution, slavery precluded the idea of men serving as protectors and maintainers. As slaves, African-American men had no means to protect themselves, let alone their wives, the women of their community, or their children."
>
> "作为一种制度，奴隶制排除了男性作为保护者和供养者的可能性。作为奴隶，非裔美国男性甚至无法保护自身，更不必说保护他们的妻子、所在社区的女性或他们的子女。"（p. 415）

### 4.2 后奴隶制时期的结构性困境

奴隶制废除后，黑人女性比男性更可能获得有薪就业，这进一步实质性地瓦解了"男性供养者"理想：

> "After slavery, during the Postbellum period, any ideal of men as 'protectors and maintainers' was further diminished simply because black women were more likely than men to be given even meagrely paid employment."
>
> "奴隶制之后，在重建时期，男性作为'保护者和供养者'的任何理想都进一步削弱了，原因很简单——黑人女性比男性更可能获得即便是微薄的有薪就业。"（p. 416）

### 4.3 齐瓦玛作为种族创伤的浪漫化补偿

非裔美国穆斯林将伊斯兰父权制作为应对黑人男性气概受侵害之历史创伤的工具：

> "African-American Muslims have used Islamic patriarchy as a construct to counter the burden of the historical legacy of assault on black masculinity and exposure of black women to white patriarchy."
>
> "非裔美国穆斯林将伊斯兰父权制用作一种建构，以对抗黑人男性气概遭受侵害以及黑人女性暴露于白人父权制这一历史遗产的重负。"（p. 416–417）

## 五、超越二元对立：第三条道路

### 5.1 北京会议的两极对立

1995年北京世界妇女大会上，穆斯林女性面临两种极化立场的困境：

> "One side believed that Islam was irretrievably patriarchal, always had been and could never change. Religion, and Islam in particular, could not be accepted as a source for women's liberation…"
>
> "一方认为伊斯兰不可挽回地是父权制的，一直如此且永远无法改变。宗教，特别是伊斯兰，不能被接受为女性解放的来源……"（p. 421）

> "The other dominant group took an Islamist stance, projecting itself as authentic, indigenous and authoritarian."
>
> "另一主导群体采取了伊斯兰主义立场，将自身投射为正统的、本土的和权威的。"（p. 421）

### 5.2 被迫在伊斯兰与人权之间选择

> "Many Muslim women were forced to choose: either Islam without full and equal rights, or human rights and feminism without Islam."
>
> "许多穆斯林女性被迫选择：要么是没有完整平等权利的伊斯兰，要么是没有伊斯兰的人权和女性主义。"（p. 424）

### 5.3 Musawah 与"亲信仰、亲女性主义"的第三立场

> "Rather than concede to either side, I chose to describe myself as 'pro-faith and pro-feminism'."
>
> "我没有向任何一方妥协，而是选择将自己定义为'亲信仰并且亲女性主义'。"（p. 423）

伊斯兰女性主义与 Musawah 组织代表了一种调和立场：

> "Musawah is one voice among several that have emerged to develop, promote and give evidence for ways of mediating between the two sides of the road to gender reform."
>
> "Musawah 是若干致力于发展、推广和论证在性别改革道路两端之间进行调和之方式的声音之一。"（p. 424）

## 六、认主独一（Tawhid）范式——教法学重构的神学基础

### 6.1 认主独一的三重维度

作者从神学原理出发，阐述认主独一的多层含义：

**第一，安拉是独一的：**

> "'*Qul huwa Allahu ahad*' ('Say: He is Allah the One,' or, 'Say: Allah is One') (Qur'an 112:1)."
>
> "'你说：他是安拉，是独一的。'（古兰经 112:1）"（p. 426）

**第二，安拉的属性在绝对和谐中统一：**

> "all attributes of Allah and their essences are in absolute harmony and unity. What may seem like contradictions adhere equally to Allah: the One Who Has Wrath is also the One of Infinite Mercy; the One Who Punishes also has Unlimited Forgiveness; the One Who Gives Life also causes Death."
>
> "安拉的一切属性及其本质处于绝对的和谐与统一之中。看似矛盾之物平等地归属安拉：拥有震怒者也是拥有无限慈悯者；惩罚者也拥有无限宽恕；赐予生命者也造成死亡。"（p. 426）

**第三，安拉是独特的，不可能有性别：**

> "'*laysa ka mithlihi shay'un*' (There is no thing like It) (42:11)… It is thus impossible for Allah to have gender. He is not male. She is not female."
>
> "'没有任何事物像他那样'（42:11）……因此安拉不可能有性别。他不是男性。她不是女性。"（p. 426）

### 6.2 认主独一的社会维度——"安拉使之合一"

> "*Tawhid* also means 'Allah unites'. That is, Allah in Her ultimate sense causes other aspects of atomic, subatomic cellular and stellar reality to *come together over seemingly irreconcilable differences*."
>
> "认主独一也意味着'安拉使之合一'。即安拉在其终极意义上使原子的、亚原子的、细胞的和星体的各种面向**跨越看似不可调和的差异走向合一**。"（p. 428）

### 6.3 认主独一作为平等原则的法律推导

> "Thus *tawhid* can serve as the operating principle of unity above hegemony, the latter resulting in dominance and oppression, while the former requires reciprocity, equality and harmony in all human relationships. The tawhidic paradigm is the counter-construct used to overcome the hierarchical terms of *qiwamah* as the basis for social interactions in intimate family relations."
>
> "因此，认主独一可以作为一种凌驾于霸权之上的统一运作原则——后者导致支配和压迫，而前者要求所有人类关系中的互惠、平等和和谐。认主独一范式是用来克服齐瓦玛作为亲密家庭关系中社会互动基础的等级化术语的反建构。"（p. 428）

### 6.4 信仰认主独一排斥社会"特殊豁免"

作者强调，信仰认主独一从逻辑上排除了在社会实践中给予任何群体的特殊豁免：

> "Belief in *tawhid* precludes anyone who identifies as Muslim from developing 'special exemptions' in the social and political applications of *tawhid*, since a core principle of this paradigm is that just and egalitarian relationships among humans are an integral part of seeking a relationship of 'engaged surrender' to God and affirming God's unity (Wadud, 2006, p. 22)."
>
> "信仰认主独一排除了任何自认为穆斯林者在认主独一的社会和政治应用中制造'特殊豁免'的可能，因为该范式的核心原则是：人与人之间公正平等的关系是寻求与安拉建立'积极归顺'关系并确认安拉独一性之不可分割的组成部分。"（p. 429）

## 七、认主独一范式的图式转换

### 7.1 传统等级模型的缺陷

传统父权模型构建了一条从安拉到男性再到女性的垂直等级线：

> "Allah, the highest metaphysical essence, was at the top. Man, or the male person, was in the middle. This position was not only in direct relationship below Allah, but also above woman, or the female person, who was at the bottom."
>
> "安拉，作为最高的形而上实在，处于顶端。男人，或男性，处于中间。这个位置不仅处于安拉之下的直接关系中，而且处于女人，或女性之上，女性处于底层。"（p. 433）

该模型的根本缺陷是切断了女性与安拉的直接关系：

> "The model does not provide direct access between the female, the Tablet or the Earth (at the bottom) and Allah – except as mediated by the male, the Pen or the Heavens in the position between her and Allah."
>
> "该模型不提供女性、笔匣或大地（在底层）与安拉之间的直接联系——除非通过处于她与安拉之间位置上的男性、笔或天穹作为中介。"（p. 434）

### 7.2 认主独一范式的重构

认主独一范式保留安拉的至上性，但将男性和女性都重新置于与安拉的直接联系中：

> "The tawhidic paradigm maintains the metaphysics of Allah's supremacy, located at the top. However, it reconstructs the diagram so that male intervention between the female and Allah is replaced. It removes the female's dependency on the male and returns her to a direct line with the divine. As a result of placing the female as well as the male – human agents before Allah – in a direct line with the divine, the human-to-human relationship can only be constructed horizontally, as one of mutual support and reciprocity."
>
> "认主独一范式保留了安拉至上性的形而上学，安拉位于顶端。然而，它重构了图式，使得男性在女性与安拉之间的干预被移除。它消除了女性对男性的依赖，将她恢复到与神圣的直接联系中。由于将女性和男性——安拉面前的人类能动者——都置于与神圣的直接联系中，人与人之间的关系便只能被水平地建构，作为一种相互支持和互惠的关系。"（p. 435）

## 八、"伊斯提克巴尔"（istikbar）——父权制作为恶魔逻辑

作者引入古兰经宇宙起源论中伊布利斯（Iblis）的叙事，将父权等级制等同于"伊斯提克巴尔"（istikbar），即恶魔式的自我优越逻辑：

> "Throughout the Qur'an, this becomes a kind of satanic logic that prevails when one group (or gender) proposes to *be better* than another group (or gender). I call this the logic of *istikbar* (from the root form of the word *kabura*), which means 'to make oneself better than another'. It was demonstrated primordially by Satan and became a moral lesson for all humans to avoid this sort of thinking. Patriarchy is not only satanic logic; it also constructs entire systems based on that logic."
>
> "贯穿古兰经，这成为一种恶魔逻辑——当一个群体（或性别）声称**优于**另一个群体（或性别）时，这种逻辑便占了上风。我将此称为'伊斯提克巴尔'（*istikbar*，源自词根 *kabura*）的逻辑，意为'使自己优于他人'。它在原初时被恶魔所展现，成为所有人类都应避免的道德教训。父权制不仅是恶魔逻辑；它还基于这种逻辑建构了整个体系。"（p. 436）

> "The Qur'an is emphatic: Allah does not do *zulm* (oppression). Thus, *zulm* is human-made and must be alleviated by human beings."
>
> "古兰经是明确的：安拉不施行'祖勒姆'（*zulm*，压迫）。因此，祖勒姆是人为的，必须由人类来消除。"（p. 436）

## 九、哈里发（Khalifah）——自由意志与性别平等的法律基础

### 9.1 人类作为安拉代理者的原初目的

> "The basic Islamic construction of what it means to be human starts with Qur'anic verse 2:30: '*inni jaa'ilun fi al-'ard khalifah*' (I will surely create on the earth a *khalifah*). The basic teleology, or divine purpose, for human creation on the earth is to be a *khalifah*."
>
> "伊斯兰关于'作为人意味着什么'的基本建构始于古兰经2:30：'我将在大地上设置一位哈里发（*khalifah*，代理者）'。人类被创造于大地之上的基本目的论或神圣目的，是成为一位哈里发。"（p. 432）

### 9.2 自由意志不可因性别而受限

> "A *khalifah* is an agent of the divine will… It means one who is free to choose, namely right from wrong, but also one who is competent to control the choices he or she makes. This free will is essential to the idea of the human being in Islam. Free will cannot be limited to male persons and then made contingent upon family for female persons – even though this is how many aspects of classical *fiqh* operate and how many Muslim patriarchal cultures developed and still practise."
>
> "哈里发是神圣意志的能动者……它意味着一个自由选择的人——即能辨别善恶，并且有能力掌控自己所做选择的人。这种自由意志对于伊斯兰中关于人的概念至关重要。自由意志不能被限定于男性，然后对女性附加家庭条件——尽管古典教法的许多方面正是如此运作的，许多穆斯林父权文化也正是如此发展和实践的。"（p. 432）

### 9.3 每个人都是安拉面前的哈里发

> "Every person, male or female, is a *khalifah* before Allah, responsible not only in a patriarchal system but beyond patriarchy in egalitarian systems. Any construct of the divine–human relation that reduces another person to a subcategory is doomed to fail."
>
> "每个人，无论男女，都是安拉面前的哈里发，不仅在父权体制中负有责任，而且在超越父权的平等体制中也负有责任。任何将他人贬为次等类别的神人关系建构注定要失败。"（p. 438）

## 十、前进之路——法律政策的范式转换

### 10.1 范式转换而非修修补补

作者强调，微调现有体系不可能实现正义，必须进行彻底的范式转换：

> "Reaching this goal can only be successful with a paradigm shift; it is not possible to adjust a little here and there without bringing down the whole house of cards."
>
> "实现这一目标只有通过范式转换才能成功；不可能在这里和那里做些微调而不使整个纸牌屋倒塌。"（p. 414）

### 10.2 认主独一伦理支撑法律政策

> "I propose a way forward in which primary Islamic theology can support policies, and these policies can be used to fulfil our ethical vision as Muslims."
>
> "我提出一条前进之路，其中伊斯兰原初神学可以支撑政策，而这些政策可以被用来实现我们作为穆斯林的伦理愿景。"（p. 430）

### 10.3 认主独一范式的普遍适用性

认主独一范式虽然起源于性别不平等的关切，但其法律和伦理含义更加广泛：

> "Although consideration of the tawhidic paradigm began with specific concerns over unequal gender relationships as encoded in Islamic jurisprudence and practised in patriarchal cultures worldwide – including among Muslims – its implications are more widespread."
>
> "尽管认主独一范式的考量始于对伊斯兰教法学所编纂的、在全球父权文化——包括穆斯林文化——中所实践的不平等性别关系的具体关切，但其含义更加广泛。"（p. 437）

### 10.4 互惠正义作为法律原则

> "By virtue of *tawhid*, a horizontal line of equality and reciprocity operates between each and the other; the same construction of human rights and substantive justice. From this Islamic perspective, justice can only be reciprocal and equal."
>
> "借助认主独一，一条平等和互惠的水平线在彼此之间运作；这与人权和实质正义的建构一致。从这种伊斯兰视角出发，正义只能是互惠的和平等的。"（p. 435）
